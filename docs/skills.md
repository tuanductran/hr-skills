# HR skills reference

All 15 skills, with descriptions and example prompts that activate each skill.

---

## hr-analytics

**What it covers:** HR metrics, dashboards, turnover analysis, predictive attrition models, data governance, privacy policies, and D&I scorecards.

**Use when you say:**

- "Analyse our employee turnover data"
- "Build an HR dashboard for the leadership team"
- "Create a predictive attrition model"
- "Write an HR data governance policy"
- "Show me which departments have the highest flight risk"

---

## hr-compensation-benefits

**What it covers:** Compensation strategy, pay analysis, market benchmarking, bonus and incentive plans, equity programs, total rewards statements, and retention strategies.

**Use when you say:**

- "Analyse our compensation data for pay equity gaps"
- "Design a sales incentive plan"
- "Write a compensation philosophy document"
- "Compare our benefits package to market"
- "Create a total rewards statement template"

## hr-compliance

- "Conduct an HR compliance audit checklist"
- "Write an ADA reasonable accommodation policy"
