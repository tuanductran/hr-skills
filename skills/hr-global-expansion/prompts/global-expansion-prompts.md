# Global expansion prompts

Use these prompts when planning people and HR infrastructure for entering a new country or region.

- "Compare entity establishment vs. EOR for hiring our first [number] employees in [country] — include cost, timeline, compliance risk, and exit flexibility."
- "Build a 12-month HR readiness timeline for entering [country], covering legal entity setup, payroll, hiring, and policy adaptation."
- "Draft a country-entry hiring strategy for [role family] in [country], including sourcing channels, compensation benchmarks, and candidate availability estimates."
- "What statutory employment benefits are required for employees in [country] and how do they compare to our current HQ package?"
- "Write a checklist of employment law requirements we must address before making our first hire in [country]."
- "Draft a local HR policy handbook section covering probation, notice periods, and termination requirements specific to [country]."
- "At what headcount threshold does switching from EOR to a local legal entity become cost-justified in [country]?"
- "Identify the top three EOR providers for [country] and compare them on coverage, compliance support, and onboarding speed."
- "Prepare a briefing for our hiring managers on cultural and management practice differences they'll encounter leading a team in [country]."
- "Design a compensation and benefits package for [role] in [country] that is locally competitive and globally consistent with our global band structure."
