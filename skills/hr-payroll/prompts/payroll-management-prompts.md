# Payroll management prompts

Use these prompts when executing payroll operations, managing tax compliance, auditing wage calculations, and handling multi-country payroll processing.

- "Draft a monthly payroll processing checklist covering timecard approvals, bonus calculations, tax withholdings, and bank file transmission deadlines."
- "Create a payroll audit protocol for validating gross-to-net calculations, statutory benefit deductions, and tax filings before final pay disbursement."
- "Write an employee communication template explaining tax form updates, pay stub layout changes, or annual statutory contribution adjustments."
- "Draft a multi-country payroll provider evaluation matrix comparing global PEO, EOR, and localized payroll processing vendors."
- "Create an overpayment recovery workflow guide detailing legal notice requirements, repayment plan templates, and payroll adjustment steps."
- "Draft a payroll compliance controls self-assessment checklist for verifying separation of duties, approval thresholds, and audit log reviews."
