# Capability assessment and development

Prompts for identifying critical capabilities, assessing current state, designing capability building programs, measuring progress, and governing capability investment.

## Identifying and prioritizing capabilities

- "Identify the top five critical capabilities [company or function] needs to develop to execute [strategic goal] over the next [timeframe]. Distinguish between foundational capabilities needed broadly and differentiating capabilities that drive competitive advantage."
- "Design a capability prioritization framework that ranks investment decisions using: strategic criticality, current gap severity, build feasibility, and business risk if unaddressed."
- "What capabilities are becoming increasingly critical in [industry] due to [AI adoption / digital transformation / market shifts]? Identify which are already gaps in a typical [function] workforce."
- "Classify our capability gaps into: build (develop internally), buy (hire), borrow (contingent/partner), or automate — and explain the key factors driving each recommendation: [paste gap list]."
- "Which capability gaps will still be unresolved in [timeframe] if we take no action beyond our current L&D programs? Identify the highest-risk ones."

## Capability assessment design

- "Design a capability assessment for [function or role family] covering [number] critical capabilities with a four-level proficiency scale and behavioral anchors at each level."
- "Build a capability heat map for [department] showing current average proficiency versus required level for the top [number] capabilities. Format it for a leadership presentation."
- "Design a manager-led capability assessment conversation guide for use in [talent review / annual review / mid-year check-in], covering: how to assess, how to validate self-ratings, and how to handle disagreements."
- "What signals in our existing performance, learning, and engagement data indicate that a capability gap is becoming a critical business risk?"
- "Design a rapid capability pulse survey for [function] that gives us directional gap data in under 15 minutes of employee time."
- "How do we build a capability assessment process for [large population] without requiring a separate self-assessment exercise? Which existing data sources can we combine?"

## Capability building program design

- "Design a capability building program for [critical capability] targeting [population size] employees over [timeframe]. Include: learning format mix, milestones, manager reinforcement, and success metrics."
- "What is the right blend of formal learning, on-the-job practice, coaching, and peer learning for building [technical / behavioral / leadership] capability in [audience]?"
- "Design a capability academy for [function or domain] with three tracks: foundational, practitioner, and expert. Include learning pathways, certification points, and a community component."
- "Design a reskilling program for [number] employees whose roles are changing due to [automation / reorganization / technology shift]. Address: communication, psychological safety, incentives for participation, and connection to job security."
- "How do we build [AI fluency / data literacy / digital skills] as a foundational capability across [non-technical population] at scale and within [budget constraint]?"
- "Design an expert community of practice for [domain] that accelerates capability spread across the organization beyond what a formal program can reach."
- "Build a capability accelerator program for [high-priority gap] that produces proficiency improvement faster than a standard learning pathway."

## Manager reinforcement and culture

- "Design a manager enablement kit for supporting [capability] development in their teams, covering: what to reinforce, how to create practice opportunities, and how to coach for capability growth."
- "Write a manager communication explaining why [capability building program] matters, what their role is, and how to have the development conversation with their team."
- "Design a set of on-the-job practice assignments for [capability] that managers can assign during regular work, without requiring dedicated time away from the job."
- "How do we create psychological safety that encourages employees to honestly acknowledge skill gaps without fear of negative consequences in [culture or team context]?"

## Measurement and impact

- "Design a capability measurement framework for [program or domain] with three levels: learning (did people complete and demonstrate knowledge?), capability (did proficiency scores improve?), and business impact (did business outcomes improve?)."
- "Build a capability progress dashboard for [CHRO / business leader] showing: gap closure rate by domain, program completion, proficiency score movement, and business impact indicators."
- "How do we measure whether our [capability program] is translating into actual performance improvement, not just learning completion?"
- "What leading indicators predict whether a capability building investment will deliver business impact within [timeframe]?"
- "Design a quarterly capability review process for [leadership team] covering: progress against plan, risks to closing priority gaps, and investment reallocation decisions."

## Governance and investment

- "Design a capability governance framework for [company] covering: who owns each critical capability, how investment decisions are made, how progress is reviewed, and how accountability is maintained."
- "Build a capability investment business case for [program or domain] showing: gap severity, business risk if unaddressed, proposed investment, expected capability outcome, and business impact."
- "Design a capability budget allocation model for L&D that prioritizes investment in the highest-gap, highest-impact capabilities rather than distributing spend equally across functions."
- "Write a capability governance charter defining: decision rights for capability investment, review cadence, success metrics, and escalation path when a critical gap is not closing."
