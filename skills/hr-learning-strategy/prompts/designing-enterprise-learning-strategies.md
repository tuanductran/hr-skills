# Designing enterprise learning strategies

Use these prompts when building, refreshing, or presenting an enterprise learning strategy for a leadership team or board.

- "Design a 3-year enterprise learning strategy for a [1,000-person/5,000-person] company, covering strategic priorities, learning ecosystem design, capability focus areas, governance, and success metrics."
- "Help me align our L&D investment plan with the three business priorities the CEO announced for this year: [AI adoption/market expansion/operational efficiency]."
- "Build a learning needs analysis framework that connects identified skill gaps to business performance data rather than relying solely on manager nominations."
- "Design a learning governance model that helps HR prioritize learning requests from business units against enterprise-wide capability priorities."
- "Create a learning budget allocation framework distinguishing between mandatory compliance training, role-critical skills development, leadership development, and discretionary learning."
- "Write a one-page learning strategy narrative for an executive team presentation that frames learning as a talent retention and business acceleration lever, not a cost center."
- "How should we measure the ROI of our leadership development program in terms business leaders will find credible, beyond completion rates and satisfaction scores?"
- "Build a skills-based learning strategy that shifts our L&D model from course completion to capability demonstration with clear proficiency milestones."
