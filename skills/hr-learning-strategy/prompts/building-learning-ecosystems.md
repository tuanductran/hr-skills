# Building learning ecosystems

Use these prompts when designing a learning technology stack, building learning pathways, or evaluating learning vendors.

- "Design a learning ecosystem architecture for a [global enterprise/fast-growth startup/regulated industry company] including LMS, content library, social learning, on-the-job learning, and mentoring components."
- "Build a vendor evaluation framework for selecting a new LMS, including evaluation criteria, demo script, and scoring rubric."
- "Create a role-based learning pathway for [Sales Account Executives/Engineering Managers/Customer Success Managers] from hire to full productivity, covering required and recommended learning at each stage."
- "Design a blended learning program for [manager capability/data literacy/AI adoption] that combines self-paced content, cohort sessions, manager coaching, and on-the-job application."
- "Build a learning content curation strategy — how should we decide what to build internally, what to license from a vendor, and what to source from free platforms?"
- "Design a manager-as-coach enablement program that helps managers turn 1:1 conversations into structured development moments without adding significant time to their schedule."
- "What learning culture signals should HR track to distinguish between an organization with high learning compliance and one with genuine capability development?"
- "Create a new hire learning journey map from pre-boarding through 6 months, covering role-specific skills, culture, tools, and relationships — not just compliance training."
