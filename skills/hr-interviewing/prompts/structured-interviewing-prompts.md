# Structured interviewing prompts

Use these prompts when building competency-based interview loops, training interview panels, creating scoring rubrics, and mitigating interviewer bias.

- "Draft a competency-based interview kit for a [role title], including behavioral STAR prompts, follow-up probes, and rating rubrics."
- "Create an interviewer calibration training guide teaching hiring panels how to evaluate candidate responses consistently and avoid cognitive biases."
- "Write a candidate scorecard rubric mapping key job requirements to 4-point rating scales with explicit behavior anchors for each score level."
- "Draft an intake kickoff agenda for recruiters and hiring managers to define required competencies, interview stage ownership, and pass thresholds."
- "Create a candidate experience interview debrief guide for facilitating productive consensus meetings without letting seniority influence ratings."
- "Draft a candidate feedback template for providing constructive, legal-compliant feedback to unsuccessful candidates."
