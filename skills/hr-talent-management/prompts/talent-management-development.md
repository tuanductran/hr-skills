# Talent Management and Development

Structured prompts for talent development, succession planning, and career path design.

## Talent development and growth planning

1. "Design a development plan for [employee type] targeting [skill/role]. Current state: [background]. Timeline: [duration]. What's the roadmap?"

   **Expected output:** Development goal, current gap analysis, learning activities and timeline, milestones, success metrics, investment required.

2. "Identify high-potential talent in [function]. What distinguishes high-potential from strong individual contributors? How do we develop them differently?"

   **Expected output:** High-potential identification criteria, development program design, mentoring/stretch assignments, timeline to readiness for next level.

## Succession planning and leadership pipeline

1. "Design succession plan for [critical role]. Current holder is [scenario]. Who's ready now? Who's 12-24 months away? Development plan?"

   **Expected output:** Successor candidates ranked by readiness, development gap analysis per candidate, mitigation if all candidates lose, contingency plan.

2. "Identify our leadership pipeline: [describe current state]. Do we have enough bench strength for growth? Where are the gaps? How do we build the bench?"

   **Expected output:** Pipeline assessment (span across org levels), gap analysis, development priorities, growth trajectory vs. internal supply.

## Career pathing and role transitions

1. "Design career path for [role] that gives upward mobility without requiring management track. What does progression look like at 3/5/10 years?"

   **Expected output:** Progression framework (individual contributor tracks, specialist roles), level definitions, skill progression, compensation bands, typical tenure at each level.

2. "Employee [description] interested in [lateral move/functional change]. Realistic? What development needed? Success probability?"

   **Expected output:** Transition feasibility assessment, skill/experience gaps, development plan, success factors, timeline.

## Performance and potential assessment

1. "Assess [employee] on 2x2 matrix (high performer / emerging talent vs. high potential / growth opportunity). What does this mean for their next move?"

   **Expected output:** Matrix positioning, implication (promote, develop, maintain, exit), tailored development approach, timeline.

2. "Design [review process / talent assessment / calibration]. How do we identify true high-potential vs. high-performer? Avoid bias?"

   **Expected output:** Assessment framework, criteria and rubrics, calibration process, bias checks, technology to support.
