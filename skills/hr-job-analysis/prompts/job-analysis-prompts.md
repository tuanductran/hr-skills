# Job analysis prompts

Use these prompts when conducting job analysis, documenting job content, or supporting classification and compensation decisions.

- "Design a job analysis interview guide for gathering task and requirement data from incumbents and supervisors for [role title]."
- "Write a job analysis questionnaire for [role family] covering essential tasks, frequency, time allocation, and required knowledge and skills."
- "Document the knowledge, skills, abilities, and other requirements (KSAOs) for [role title] based on the following task and duty information."
- "Write the essential functions and marginal functions for [role title] for ADA compliance documentation purposes."
- "Evaluate the complexity of [role] using a point-factor job evaluation methodology — assess knowledge, problem-solving, accountability, and working conditions."
- "Design a job family classification framework for [function] covering levels from entry to expert, including distinguishing criteria between levels."
- "How do we defend a job classification outcome to a manager who believes their role should be graded higher?"
- "Build a job audit process to verify whether existing job descriptions accurately reflect current role content after two years of organizational change."
- "Write a task analysis for [operational role] that identifies which tasks could be restructured, automated, or reassigned as part of a job redesign."
- "Design a job analysis validation process to ensure the data collected from incumbents reflects actual work rather than idealized or inflated job content."
