# Labor relations prompts

Use these prompts when managing collective bargaining, grievance processes, workplace investigations, or labor relations risk in union and non-union environments.

- "Help me build a collective bargaining preparation package for [upcoming contract negotiations] covering our current cost position, the union's likely priority issues, our walk-away points, our opening position, and the internal approval process for concessions."
- "Design a contract administration process for [company] that tells managers how to handle the five most common day-to-day situations arising under [the new collective bargaining agreement] — without requiring HR involvement at every step."
- "An employee has filed a formal grievance alleging that management violated [specific contract clause]. Walk me through the grievance investigation steps, the documentation I need to produce, and how to prepare for the grievance hearing."
- "Build a manager training module covering what managers can and cannot say or do during a union organizing campaign — using the TIPS framework and specific examples of lawful and unlawful conduct."
- "Design a labor relations risk assessment for [company / business unit] that identifies the top three labor relations exposures — covering organizing risk, contract compliance gaps, and pending or likely grievances — and recommends mitigation steps for each."
- "How do I evaluate whether an interest-based bargaining approach or a traditional positional approach better fits [our current labor relations climate] — and what are the trade-offs of each from a legal, cost, and relationship perspective?"
- "Design a workplace investigation protocol for a [union environment / non-union environment] that meets our legal obligations, protects both parties, and produces a documentation record that would withstand arbitration."
- "Write manager talking points for communicating [a policy change / a contract implementation update / a workforce restructuring] to a unionized workforce — in a way that is legally compliant under the NLRA and does not trigger an unfair labor practice charge."
- "Help me design a post-contract implementation review process for [90 days after ratification] that checks whether the agreement is being applied consistently across supervisors and identifies early grievance patterns before they escalate."
- "What are the most common NLRA compliance failures made by supervisors during a [union organizing campaign / grievance process / disciplinary action], and how do I build a rapid-response manager briefing that prevents them?"
