# HR Employee Lifecycle

## Supported tasks

- Mapping and documenting the end-to-end employee journey
- Designing employee experience touchpoints at each lifecycle stage
- Creating lifecycle personas for different employee segments
- Identifying and closing gaps in the employee experience
- Developing lifecycle-based communication plans
- Designing surveys and feedback mechanisms for each lifecycle stage
- Building lifecycle dashboards to track attrition, engagement, and productivity
- Aligning lifecycle management with employer branding strategy
- Creating playbooks for HR business partners across lifecycle stages
- Benchmarking lifecycle practices against industry standards

## Key prompts

### Journey mapping

1. "Map the employee journey for a [role/level] from offer acceptance to 90 days."
2. "Identify the key moments that matter across the employee lifecycle for [industry]."
3. "Create an employee journey map template with emotional highs and lows."
4. "Design lifecycle touchpoints for a remote-first workforce."
5. "Build a lifecycle framework covering attract, hire, onboard, develop, retain, and exit."

### Experience design

1. "Design a consistent employee experience framework across all lifecycle stages."
2. "Create a moments-that-matter inventory for [company type]."
3. "Develop lifecycle-based HR communications for [employee segment]."
4. "Identify pain points in the employee lifecycle through data analysis prompts."
5. "Create a lifecycle improvement roadmap based on [survey findings]."

### Measurement and analytics

1. "Build an employee lifecycle metrics framework with leading indicators."
2. "Design a lifecycle health dashboard for HR leadership reporting."
3. "Create lifecycle segment analysis to identify at-risk employee groups."
4. "Develop a cohort retention analysis framework by hire year."
5. "Generate survey questions to measure experience quality at each lifecycle stage."

### HR business partner enablement

1. "Create an HR business partner playbook for managing lifecycle transitions."
2. "Develop manager guides for supporting employees at each lifecycle stage."
3. "Write lifecycle-aware conversation guides for 1:1 check-ins."
4. "Design escalation protocols for lifecycle risk signals."

## Tips

- Focus on moments that matter most—not every interaction has equal impact on the employee experience.
- Collect feedback at transitions (hire, promotion, role change, departure) to surface actionable insights.
- Segment lifecycle analysis by tenure, role family, and location to uncover hidden patterns.
- Involve managers early—they own the day-to-day lifecycle experience more than HR does.
- Treat the alumni stage as part of the lifecycle; former employees are brand ambassadors and rehire candidates.
- Connect lifecycle data to business outcomes like productivity and customer satisfaction.
