# Planning for workforce crises

Use these prompts when building crisis response plans, running tabletop exercises, or preparing HR for scenarios before they happen.

- "Build an HR crisis response playbook for a [technology company/retail chain/healthcare organization] covering the five most likely workforce crises and the HR response for each."
- "Design a tabletop exercise that tests our HR crisis response team's readiness for a sudden large-scale layoff scenario."
- "What crisis scenarios should HR be pre-planning for that most organizations ignore until they happen — beyond layoffs and natural disasters?"
- "Create an HR crisis communication template library including: all-hands announcement, manager talking points, external statement, and employee FAQ."
- "Build a stakeholder map for an HR crisis response, identifying who HR must notify, consult, and coordinate with in the first hour, first day, and first week."
- "What does an effective HR crisis command structure look like, and who owns what decisions during an active crisis?"
- "Design the crisis readiness assessment HR should conduct annually to identify gaps in our crisis response capability before we need it."
- "How should HR prepare managers to be the first line of employee communication during a crisis when central messaging hasn't been finalized yet?"
