# HR Automation

## Supported tasks

- Identifying and prioritizing HR process automation opportunities
- Designing automated workflows for onboarding, offboarding, and lifecycle events
- Building HRIS automation configurations and integration specifications
- Creating HR chatbot and virtual assistant use case designs
- Developing AI-assisted HR content generation frameworks
- Mapping current-state HR processes for automation readiness assessment
- Writing business cases for HR automation investments
- Designing data quality frameworks to support automation reliability
- Creating change management plans for HR automation rollouts
- Measuring automation ROI through time savings and error reduction metrics

## Key prompts

### Automation opportunity identification

1. "Identify the top 10 HR processes with the highest automation potential in [company context]."
2. "Create an HR process automation prioritization matrix based on volume, complexity, and impact."
3. "Conduct an HR admin burden assessment to identify time-intensive manual tasks."
4. "Map the onboarding process and flag automation opportunities at each step."
5. "Develop an HR automation readiness assessment for [HRIS/data quality/process maturity]."

### Workflow design

1. "Design an automated onboarding workflow from offer acceptance to day-one readiness."
2. "Build a lifecycle event automation map for [promotion/transfer/departure] processes."
3. "Create an automated leave management workflow integrating approval, notification, and payroll."
4. "Design a document generation automation for [offer letters/contracts/policies]."
5. "Develop an automated HR reporting workflow for weekly and monthly dashboards."

### AI and technology in HR

1. "Design an HR chatbot use case covering [FAQs/leave queries/policy questions]."
2. "Create a framework for using AI in [resume screening/interview scheduling/onboarding content]."
3. "Develop guidelines for responsible AI use in HR processes."
4. "Write an HRIS automation specification for [Workday/SAP/BambooHR] configuration."
5. "Design an AI-assisted HR content workflow for job descriptions, communications, and policies."

### Business case and ROI

1. "Write a business case for investing in HR automation for [specific process]."
2. "Develop an HR automation ROI model tracking time saved, error reduction, and employee experience."
3. "Create a total cost of ownership framework for HR automation tools."
4. "Build an HR automation roadmap from quick wins to strategic transformation."
5. "Design a KPI framework for measuring HR automation program success."

## Tips

- Start automation with high-volume, rule-based processes—they deliver the fastest ROI and lowest implementation risk.
- Clean data is a prerequisite for reliable automation; audit data quality before building automated workflows.
- Involve employees and managers in automation design to ensure usability and avoid creating new friction.
- Apply responsible AI principles to any automated HR decision-making, especially in hiring and performance.
- Automate the task, not the relationship—use automation to free HR capacity for complex human interactions.
- Measure time saved and error reduction post-implementation to validate automation investment and justify further scope.
