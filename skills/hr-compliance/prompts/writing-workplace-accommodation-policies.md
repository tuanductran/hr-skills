# Writing workplace accommodation policies

This AI assistant can be a valuable tool for writing workplace accommodation policies. With its natural language processing capabilities, it can generate helpful prompts and templates to guide you in crafting policies that are compliant with legal regulations and promote inclusivity in the workplace. You can provide the assistant with relevant information and it will provide you with a starting point for your policy.

- "Can you help me draft a workplace accommodation policy for [employees with disabilities] that takes into consideration their specific needs, such as [mobility impairments, visual impairments, etc.], and provides [assistive technology, ergonomic accommodations, etc.] as necessary?"
- "I need to create a policy that outlines how [employees with medical conditions] can request reasonable accommodations, including [flexible scheduling, job restructuring, medical leave, etc.], and the documentation they need to provide. Can you provide me with a template that is compliant with [ADA, FMLA, etc.] regulations?"
- "I want to make sure my workplace accommodation policy is inclusive of [pregnant employees], including those with [high-risk pregnancies, gestational diabetes, etc.]. Can you help me write a section on this topic that provides accommodations such as [more frequent breaks, modified duties, etc.] and outlines the [documentation, timeline, etc.] needed for leave?"
- "I need to create a policy for [employees with religious beliefs] that allows them to observe their religious practices while at work, including [daily prayers, dietary restrictions, etc.]. Can you give me some language to use that balances the rights of the employee with the needs of the business, while also being compliant with [Title VII, EEOC, etc.] guidelines?"
- "Can you provide me with a template for a workplace accommodation policy that is applicable to [all employees], taking into consideration [diverse backgrounds, cultures, and languages], and provides [language interpretation services, diverse hiring practices, etc.] to ensure inclusivity and equity?"
