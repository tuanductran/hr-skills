# Global HR operations prompts

Use these prompts when designing or operating HR programs that span multiple countries.

- "Design a global HR operating model for a company with entities in [countries], distinguishing center-of-excellence functions from local HRBP responsibilities."
- "Create a country HR compliance checklist covering contracts, statutory benefits, notice periods, and data privacy requirements for [country]."
- "Write a global HR policy framework that sets non-negotiable global standards while giving local teams flexibility to adapt for legal and cultural fit."
- "Draft an expatriate policy covering pre-departure support, cost-of-living adjustments, housing allowance, home leave, and repatriation."
- "Develop a short-term international assignment framework for deployments of 3-12 months, including tax and social security treatment."
- "Build a global termination process guide accounting for local notice requirements, severance obligations, and consultation requirements in [country list]."
- "Write a cross-border hiring decision guide covering when to use a local entity, EOR, or independent contractor for a new market hire."
- "Design a cultural onboarding guide for employees joining a team based in [country], covering communication norms, working hours, and feedback culture."
- "Create a global mobility decision matrix for choosing between permanent transfer, long-term assignment, short-term assignment, and commuter arrangement."
- "Write manager guidance for leading distributed teams across [specific country pairing] — covering communication rhythms, meeting time zones, and feedback norms."
