# Digitizing and automating HR processes

Use these prompts when identifying processes for digitization, designing workflow automation, or building self-service HR experiences.

- "Map the current state of our [onboarding/offboarding/performance review] process and identify which steps should be digitized, automated, or eliminated."
- "Design an employee self-service portal experience that allows employees to manage [leave requests/benefits enrollment/personal data updates] without HR involvement."
- "Write the workflow automation specification for our offer letter generation process, including the triggers, data inputs, approvals, and output documents."
- "Build a manager self-service checklist covering the 10 HR tasks managers should be able to complete without contacting HR directly."
- "Design a digital onboarding experience that completes all administrative tasks before day one so the new hire's first day focuses on connection and context."
- "What HR workflows are best suited for robotic process automation (RPA), and what are the risks of automating them without a strong data foundation first?"
- "Create a process redesign brief for our annual performance review cycle — identify the steps that add value and the steps that create administrative burden without improving outcomes."
- "Design the notification and reminder system for our HRIS so employees and managers receive timely prompts for open enrollment, review deadlines, and compliance requirements."
