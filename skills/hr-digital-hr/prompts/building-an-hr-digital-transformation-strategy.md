# Building an HR digital transformation strategy

Use these prompts when designing or executing a digital HR transformation, building an HR tech stack strategy, or creating a digital HR roadmap.

- "Create a 12-month HR digital transformation roadmap for a [500-person/2,000-person] company that currently runs HR on spreadsheets and email, prioritizing quick wins alongside foundational investments."
- "Design an HR technology stack for a [fast-growth startup/enterprise company/multi-country operation] covering core HRIS, ATS, LMS, performance management, and employee engagement tools."
- "Build a vendor evaluation framework for selecting a new HRIS, including evaluation criteria, demo script questions, reference check questions, and decision scoring."
- "What are the HR processes we should automate first to deliver the fastest ROI, and what tools or platforms are typically used to automate each?"
- "Design a digital employee experience audit — what are the HR digital touchpoints an employee encounters from offer to offboarding, and how do we assess their quality?"
- "Create a digital HR change management plan for a company migrating from legacy HR software to a modern cloud HRIS, including manager and employee enablement."
- "What governance model should we put in place to manage our HR tech stack — who owns platform decisions, data standards, and vendor relationships?"
- "Build a data-driven HR operating model framework: what data does HR need to collect, how should it flow between systems, and what dashboards should it produce?"
