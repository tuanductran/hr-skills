# HR Project Management Prompts

- "Draft a project charter for migrating from [old HR system] to [new HR system]."
- "Create a RACI chart for an HRIS implementation involving HR, IT, and payroll teams."
- "Build a risk register with 8 likely risks for a company-wide policy rollout."
- "Draft a go-live readiness checklist for launching a new benefits enrollment system."
- "Create a weekly status report template with sections for progress, risks, and decisions needed."
- "Draft a lessons-learned template covering what went well, what didn't, and recommendations."
