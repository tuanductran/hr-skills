# HR training and development

Comprehensive learning and development support — from needs assessments and training design to e-learning, coaching programs, mentorship, competency models, and learning assessments.

## Supported tasks

- Conducting learning needs assessments
- Creating and designing training materials and e-learning courses
- Developing coaching and mentorship programs
- Writing competency models and career development plans
- Designing on-the-job and blended learning experiences
- Developing microlearning modules and gamified training
- Conducting train-the-trainer sessions
- Writing performance review templates and learning objectives
- Creating employee handbooks and orientation materials
- Developing digital badges and credentials programs

## Key prompts

### Needs assessment

1. "How should I conduct a learning needs assessment for [department/role] to identify the most critical skill gaps?"
2. "What questions should be included in a training needs analysis survey for [team/function]?"
3. "How can I use performance data and manager input to prioritize training investments?"
4. "What are the key steps to conducting a thorough organizational training needs assessment?"

### Training design and materials

1. "Create a training outline for [topic] for [audience, for example, new managers, customer service reps, sales team]."
2. "What are the key components of effective training materials for [subject area]?"
3. "How should I design an engaging on-the-job training experience for [role]?"
4. "Write learning objectives for a training program on [topic] using Bloom's Taxonomy."
5. "How can I create training materials that accommodate different learning styles (visual, auditory, kinesthetic)?"
6. "Design a gamified training experience for [topic] that increases learner engagement and knowledge retention."

### E-Learning and digital learning

1. "How should I design an e-learning course on [topic] that is engaging and effective for remote learners?"
2. "What are best practices for developing microlearning modules on [topic] for busy employees?"
3. "How can I create a blended learning strategy that combines online and in-person training for [program]?"
4. "What platforms and tools are best suited for delivering [type of training] to our workforce?"
5. "How can virtual reality training simulations be used to train employees on [high-risk/complex task]?"
6. "How should I develop digital badges and credentials to recognize learning achievement?"

### Coaching and mentorship

1. "Design a coaching plan for [employee name] who wants to develop [specific skill/competency]."
2. "What are the key elements of an effective mentorship program for [new hires/high-potentials/specific group]?"
3. "How should I structure a mentorship matching process to ensure productive mentor-mentee pairs?"
4. "Write a coaching session agenda for a manager working on improving [leadership skill]."
5. "What are best practices for conducting train-the-trainer sessions that build internal training capability?"

### Competency models and career development

1. "Develop a competency model for [role/function] that defines the key skills, knowledge, and behaviors for success."
2. "Write a career development plan for [employee name] who aspires to move into [target role]."
3. "How can I create personalized learning plans that align with both employee goals and business needs?"
4. "What framework should I use to design a leadership competency model for our organization?"
5. "How can I develop a job rotation plan that builds broad skills and prepares employees for advancement?"

### Onboarding training

1. "Design a manager onboarding training program that covers [key topics: people management, company culture, systems]."
2. "What should be included in an onboarding training curriculum for new employees in [role]?"
3. "How can I create an engaging orientation program that helps new hires feel welcomed and prepared?"
4. "Write an employee handbook outline that covers policies, culture, and essential information for new hires."

### Assessment and measurement

1. "How should I write a learning assessment report that demonstrates the ROI of a training program?"
2. "What metrics should I use to evaluate training effectiveness using Kirkpatrick's four levels?"
3. "Design a post-training feedback survey that captures learner reactions and self-assessed learning."
4. "How can I write a learning transfer plan that helps employees apply training back on the job?"
5. "What approaches help measure behavior change after completing a leadership development program?"

## Tips

- Always start with a needs assessment before designing training — avoid training for the wrong problem.
- Use the 70-20-10 model: 70% on-the-job learning, 20% coaching/mentoring, 10% formal training.
- Write specific, measurable learning objectives before designing any content.
- Measure training impact at all four Kirkpatrick levels — especially behavior change and business results.
- Make learning continuous, not a one-time event — follow up with coaching, job aids, and practice opportunities.
