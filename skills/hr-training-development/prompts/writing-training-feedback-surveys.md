# Writing training feedback surveys

This AI assistant can be a valuable tool in helping you write training feedback surveys. As a language model trained on a large corpus of text, it can provide you with a variety of prompts and ideas to guide you through the survey-writing process. By generating sample questions, phrases, and responses, it can help you create surveys that are clear, concise, and effective at collecting feedback from your trainees.

- "Can you generate feedback survey questions for a [specific training program] that is focused on [specific skills or topic]? I would like the questions to address [specific aspects of the training, such as trainer performance, content, or participant engagement]."
- "Could you provide me with questions that can be used to gather feedback on the [trainer's performance] during a [specific training program]? Specifically, I'm interested in how the trainer [engaged participants, delivered the material, etc.]."
- "I'm looking for questions that can help me assess [participant engagement] in the [specific training program]. Can you provide me with questions that address [specific ways to measure engagement, such as participation in activities, level of interaction with the trainer, etc.]?"
- "Can you generate questions that are aimed at improving the [effectiveness of the training program] in terms of [specific goals or outcomes]? I want to ensure that the training is [meeting the needs of the participants, producing measurable results, etc.]."
- "Could you provide me with questions that can help me identify [areas for improvement] in the [specific training program]? I want to make sure that future iterations of the training address [specific issues or concerns raised by participants]."
