# Talent mapping prompts

Use these prompts when mapping candidate landscapes, identifying high-potential talent in target organizations, and building executive talent pipelines.

- "Draft a competitive talent mapping brief identifying top engineering leaders across [target companies] in [geographic region]."
- "Create an organizational chart research guide for mapping team structures and reporting lines within competitor organizations."
- "Write candidate profiling templates for capturing career trajectories, technical achievements, and mobility indicators."
- "Draft a talent pipeline tracking sheet for categorizing mapped candidates by readiness level, domain expertise, and outreach status."
- "Create a talent mapping intake questionnaire for hiring managers to specify target candidate profiles and source company lists."
- "Draft a talent landscape presentation outline for executive sponsors showing candidate density across key markets."
