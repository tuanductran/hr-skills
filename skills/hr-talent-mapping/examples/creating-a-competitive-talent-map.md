# Creating a competitive talent map

## Context

An executive search team needs to map Vice President of Engineering talent across Series B and C technology companies to prepare for upcoming succession needs and key executive hires.

## Step 1: Define search parameters and target company list

Sample prompt: "Build a target company list and candidate criteria profile for a VP of Engineering talent mapping initiative."

Expected response: A list of 15-20 target growth-stage companies with matching domain characteristics, defining target titles, experience thresholds, and key technical capabilities.

## Step 2: Build candidate profiles and talent map

Sample prompt: "Draft a candidate mapping template capturing candidate current role, tenure, previous employers, public contributions, and estimated readiness."

Expected response: A structured talent matrix profiling mapped executive candidates, categorizing them into immediate targets, long-term nurture candidates, and key industry influencers.

## Step 3: Develop engagement strategy and pipeline tracking

Sample prompt: "Draft a discreet relationship-building outreach plan for engaging mapped executive candidates over a 6-month period."

Expected response: A multi-touch networking cadence focused on relationship building, market updates, and low-friction introductions rather than immediate job pitches.

## Workflow summary

The recruiter defines target criteria, systematically maps candidate profiles across competitor organizations, and maintains a warm executive talent pipeline for future leadership needs.
