# Understanding QA and software testing for HR

Use these prompts when you need to understand QA engineering concepts well enough to screen candidates, evaluate portfolios, or advise hiring managers.

- "Explain [shift-left testing/test-driven development/behavior-driven development] in plain terms so I can discuss them confidently with QA candidates."
- "What is the difference between [E2E testing, integration testing, unit testing, and API testing], and what does it tell me about a candidate's depth if they can explain all four?"
- "A candidate said they use [page object model/test fixtures/data-driven testing]. Is that a strong signal for a senior automation role?"
- "What does [CI/CD integration for test suites] mean in a QA context, and why is it important for evaluating senior automation engineers?"
- "Explain [performance testing with k6 or JMeter/load testing/stress testing] in simple terms — what does it mean if a QA candidate has this experience?"
- "What does [WCAG accessibility testing] mean, and when would a company ask a QA engineer to own accessibility compliance testing?"
- "A QA candidate mentioned [contract testing with Pact/consumer-driven contracts]. What is this and why might a senior candidate mention it?"
- "What are the most important QA engineering terms a technical recruiter should know when building a sourcing pipeline for [automation/SDET/quality engineering] roles?"
