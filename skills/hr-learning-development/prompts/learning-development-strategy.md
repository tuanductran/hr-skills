# Learning and Development Strategy

Structured prompts for designing learning programs, L&D strategy, and talent development initiatives.

## L&D strategy and program design

1. "Design L&D strategy for [organization]. Current state: [challenges]. Priorities: [business needs]. What learning programs should we build?"

   **Expected output:** Learning strategy aligned to business priorities, program portfolio (onboarding, technical skills, leadership), delivery modality mix (instructor-led, online, coaching), investment/ROI.

2. "We need to build [skill] across [team/org]. What's the learning journey? Who trains? How do we measure success? Timeline and cost?"

   **Expected output:** Learning design (curriculum, modules, sequencing), delivery approach, instructor/facilitator needs, success metrics, timeline and investment.

## Onboarding and ramp-up programs

1. "Design onboarding for [new hire type: IC, manager, executive]. What should happen day 1, week 1, month 1, month 3? Success metrics?"

   **Expected output:** Onboarding timeline and milestones, key activities per phase, stakeholder roles, technology/resources needed, ramp-up metrics (productivity, retention).

2. "New hire not ramping up fast enough. Current progress: [description]. Root cause? Intervention? Extended onboarding? Exit?"

   **Expected output:** Ramp diagnostic, root cause analysis, intervention options (mentoring, different assignments, additional training, support), timeline, go/no-go decision.

## Technical skills and continuous learning

1. "Design continuous learning program for [skill area: leadership, technical, product]. Budget: [amount]. Cadence: [timing]. How do we deliver?"

   **Expected output:** Program design (topics, formats, frequency), delivery modality (internal trainer, external vendor, self-paced), enrollment strategy, participation metrics, refresher cadence.

2. "Assess readiness for [certification/advanced skill]. [Employee] wants to pursue [certification]. Should we support? Cost? Timeline? ROI?"

   **Expected output:** Certification relevance assessment, employee readiness evaluation, cost-benefit analysis, study support options, post-cert utilization plan.

## Learning culture and engagement

1. "Engagement with L&D programs is low. Current participation: [numbers]. Barriers? How do we increase adoption and engagement?"

   **Expected output:** Barrier analysis (time, relevance, format, culture, awareness), adoption levers (incentives, leadership role modeling, integration with career growth), engagement metrics to track.

2. "Build business case for [L&D investment: learning platform, coaching, external training]. Expected ROI? Alternatives? How to justify?"

   **Expected output:** Investment business case, expected outcomes (skill lift, retention, performance improvement), ROI assumptions and calculations, alternatives and comparison, recommendation.
