# Talent Acquisition Strategy and Operations — Prompts

1. "Design a TA strategy for [company type, size]. We need to hire [count] people in [timeframe]. Current sourcing: [methods]. How do we scale recruiting without sacrificing quality?"

   **Expected output:** TA operating model recommendation, sourcing channel strategy, ATS setup, SLA framework, recruiter team sizing, timeline and investment.

2. "Build a recruiting SLA framework for [company]. We want [time-to-fill target]. What stages should we optimize? What's realistic given our sourcing channels?"

   **Expected output:** Stage-by-stage SLA targets, process changes to hit targets, identifying bottlenecks, resource needs, realistic achievable timeline.

3. "Design a talent sourcing strategy for [hard-to-fill role] in [market]. Market is [tight/moderate/abundant]. What mix of channels gives us [volume target] at [quality target]?"

   **Expected output:** Channel strategy (inbound/direct/referral/agency mix), resource allocation, expected volume and quality per channel, timeline to build pipeline.

4. "Set up ATS workflows for [hiring volume, functions]. What stages? What automation? How do we track metrics?"

   **Expected output:** ATS stage design, integration points, automation rules, reporting dashboards, recruiter training plan.

5. "Measure TA effectiveness: we fill [X] hires at [Y] cost in [Z] days. Is this good? Benchmark? Where are our inefficiencies?"

   **Expected output:** Benchmark assessment (vs. industry), efficiency diagnosis (where time/cost is high), improvement opportunities with ROI.

6. "Build business case for TA investment: we want [tools/team/program]. Cost: [X]. Expected benefit: [claim]. How do we prove ROI?"

   **Expected output:** ROI assumptions, measurement framework, baseline metrics needed, business case with conservative/optimistic scenarios.

7. "Design partnership model between TA and hiring managers. Current state: [issues]. How do we align incentives and improve communication?"

   **Expected output:** Hiring manager education program, recruiter roles and accountabilities, SLAs both directions, communication cadence, feedback loops.

8. "Audit our recruiting process for [issue: slow, low quality, candidate complaints]. Root cause? Recommendations?"

   **Expected output:** Diagnosis of problem areas, data supporting diagnosis, specific process changes, expected improvement, implementation timeline.
