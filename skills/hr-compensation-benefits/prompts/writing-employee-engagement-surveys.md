# Writing employee engagement surveys

This AI assistant can be a valuable resource for writing employee engagement surveys. With its vast knowledge base and natural language processing capabilities, it can help generate prompts for survey questions, provide insights on what questions to ask and how to structure the survey, and even help analyze the data collected from the survey.

- "Can you provide me with a list of survey questions for measuring employee engagement in the [industry] sector that focuses on [customer service/innovation/diversity]?"
- "I need help structuring my employee engagement survey. Can you suggest a format that works best for [small/medium/large] organizations with [centralized/decentralized/ hybrid] team structures that operate in [domestic/international] markets?"
- "What are some tips for writing effective employee engagement questions that elicit honest responses from [new/tenured/virtual/remote/underrepresented] employees with [disabilities/mental health challenges/ language barriers]?"
- "Can you help me analyze the results of my employee engagement survey and identify areas for improvement in [communication/management/leadership/organizational culture/work-life balance] specifically for [front-line staff/managers/executives]?"
- "I want to make sure my employee engagement survey is inclusive of all employees. Can you suggest some ways to make the questions more inclusive for [diverse/remote/overseas/ multigenerational] teams with [disabilities/languages/ethnicities/gender identities/sexual orientations] and working in [shift-based roles/part-time roles/ contract roles]?"
