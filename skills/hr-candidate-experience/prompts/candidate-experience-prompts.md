# Candidate experience prompts

Use these prompts when mapping candidate touchpoints, improving communication transparency, gathering feedback, and optimizing candidate satisfaction.

- "Draft candidate communication templates for stage updates, interview preparation guides, and decision notifications."
- "Create a candidate experience survey questionnaire measuring satisfaction with recruiter responsiveness and interview clarity."
- "Write an interview preparation packet for candidates outlining company values, interview agenda, and panel bios."
- "Draft a rejection letter template providing constructive, respectful feedback to candidates after final round interviews."
- "Create a candidate journey audit checklist identifying friction points from application submission to offer acceptance."
- "Draft an internal candidate communication policy establishing response SLAs for recruiters and hiring managers."
