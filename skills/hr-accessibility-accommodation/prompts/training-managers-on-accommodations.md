# Training managers on accommodations

Use these prompts when developing manager training, guidance documents, or coaching materials on supporting workplace accommodations.

- "Design a 30-minute manager training module on recognizing accommodation requests even when an employee doesn't use formal language or cite a specific law."
- "Write a manager FAQ covering the five most common accommodation scenarios they'll encounter — remote work, flexible hours, ergonomic equipment, meeting format changes, and leave adjustments."
- "Draft a one-page reference card for managers on what to say, what not to say, and what to do when an employee mentions a health condition that may affect their work."
- "Create a scenario-based roleplay exercise helping managers practice the first conversation after an accommodation request, without asking medical questions they shouldn't ask."
- "Write guidance for a manager whose team member has an approved accommodation that other team members are questioning as unfair treatment."
- "What should managers document versus what should they never document when supporting an employee's accommodation?"
- "Draft a 90-day check-in script a manager can use to assess whether an approved accommodation is working effectively without reopening the medical conversation."
