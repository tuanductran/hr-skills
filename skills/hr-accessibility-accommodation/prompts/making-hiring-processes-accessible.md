# Making hiring processes accessible

Use these prompts when auditing or redesigning recruiting, interviewing, and onboarding for accessibility.

- "Audit our current interview process for accessibility barriers and recommend default accommodations we should offer all candidates at scheduling."
- "Write a candidate-facing accommodation request prompt to include in interview invitations that makes it easy to request support without stigma."
- "Design an accessible virtual interview setup checklist covering platform features, lighting, captioning, and screen reader compatibility."
- "What physical accessibility considerations apply to [on-site interviews/assessment centers/new hire orientation] for candidates with mobility limitations?"
- "Create a sourcing and outreach strategy that reaches candidates with disabilities through channels beyond standard job boards."
- "Rewrite our standard skills assessment for a [data analyst/customer support/software engineer] role to offer three equivalent format options."
- "Draft guidance for hiring managers on how to handle an in-interview accommodation request they were not informed about in advance."
- "What default job description language should we adopt to signal accessibility commitment without creating legal ambiguity about essential functions?"
