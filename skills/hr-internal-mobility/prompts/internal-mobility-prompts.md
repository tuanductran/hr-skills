# Internal mobility prompts

Use these prompts when designing or running internal mobility programs, processes, and conversations.

- "Design an internal mobility framework for a [company size] organization, including eligibility criteria, application process, and manager approval workflow."
- "Write an internal job posting policy covering minimum tenure requirements, eligibility rules, posting timelines, and the internal vs. external posting sequence."
- "Create a manager guide for releasing employees to internal opportunities — including how to have the conversation, timeline expectations, and how to plan for the backfill."
- "Draft conversation guides for managers responding to an employee who expresses interest in moving to a different team or function."
- "Build an internal talent marketplace model for project-based mobility, including how employees express interest, how managers post projects, and how matches are made."
- "Design a rotation program structure for [early career / high-potential] employees, covering rotation length, host team responsibilities, and performance evaluation."
- "Create an internal mobility metrics dashboard covering internal fill rate, time-to-fill for internal moves, internal vs. external hire performance, and retention uplift."
- "Write an employee self-assessment tool for identifying internal mobility readiness, covering skills, career goals, and tenure."
- "Draft an internal mobility communication plan explaining the program to employees and managers at launch."
- "Design a career lattice framework for [function] that maps lateral and cross-functional growth paths alongside traditional vertical progression."
