# Recruitment operations prompts

Use these prompts when optimizing recruiting workflows, configuring ATS automation, managing recruiting vendors, and measuring pipeline health.

- "Draft an ATS workflow optimization guide mapping stage definitions, automated candidate status emails, and interviewer assignment rules."
- "Create a recruiting capacity model template for calculating recruiter bandwidth based on active requisitions, historical time-to-fill, and target hiring goals."
- "Write a talent acquisition vendor management playbook establishing agency SLA standards, fee structures, and performance review schedules."
- "Draft a interview scheduling automation protocol detailing calendar sync rules, candidate self-scheduling links, and reminder cadences."
- "Create a recruiting pipeline health dashboard specification tracking funnel conversion rates, stage velocity, and sourcing channel ROI."
- "Draft a candidate background check and compliance verification workflow ensuring data privacy, consent tracking, and adverse action protocols."
