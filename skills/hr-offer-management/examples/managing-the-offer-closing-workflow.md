# Managing the offer closing workflow

## Context

A recruiter needs to extend a complex job offer to a Senior Staff Engineer candidate who has competing offers and concerns regarding equity vesting timelines.

## Step 1: Prepare the offer package and total rewards summary

Sample prompt: "Draft a total rewards presentation breakdown highlighting base salary, performance bonus, equity value, and health benefits."

Expected response: A comprehensive total rewards summary visual breakdown illustrating first-year target earnings, 4-year equity vesting schedules, and monetary value of company benefits.

## Step 2: Deliver verbal offer and address concerns

Sample prompt: "Write a verbal offer closing script addressing candidate concerns about competing offers and equity vesting schedules."

Expected response: A collaborative conversation guide helping recruiters present the offer enthusiastically, probe for underlying decision drivers, and address equity questions transparently.

## Step 3: Formalize offer documentation and pre-onboarding

Sample prompt: "Draft an offer acceptance confirmation letter and 30-day pre-onboarding check-in schedule."

Expected response: Formal offer agreement paperwork and a structured pre-onboarding touchpoint cadence keeping the candidate connected with the team prior to day one.

## Workflow summary

The recruiter prepares a transparent total rewards summary, conducts a structured offer conversation addressing competing offers, and maintains candidate connection through formal pre-onboarding.
