# Offer management prompts

Use these prompts when structuring compensation offers, managing offer closing conversations, drafting offer letter templates, and mitigating counter-offer risks.

- "Draft an offer presentation script for recruiters delivering verbal offers to senior technical candidates."
- "Create an offer letter template containing standard employment terms, compensation breakdown, equity details, and benefit summaries."
- "Write a counter-offer coaching framework for recruiters discussing candidate concerns and handling competing job offers."
- "Draft a total rewards summary sheet template detailing base salary, performance bonuses, equity vesting schedules, and wellness perks."
- "Create an offer approval matrix defining approval thresholds for salary band exceptions, sign-on bonuses, and custom perk requests."
- "Draft a post-offer candidate pre-onboarding engagement schedule to maintain excitement during notice periods."
