# HR coaching and mentoring

Build coaching and mentoring capability across your organization — from designing structured mentoring programs and training managers in coaching skills to facilitating individual coaching conversations using proven frameworks like GROW and CLEAR.

## Supported tasks

- Designing formal mentoring program structures and matching processes
- Building manager coaching capability through frameworks and training
- Creating coaching conversation guides using GROW, CLEAR, and similar models
- Developing coaching culture assessments and maturity roadmaps
- Writing peer coaching program designs for team-level application
- Creating coach and mentor selection and onboarding materials
- Designing group coaching and cohort learning experiences
- Building coach/mentor training curricula for internal facilitators
- Measuring coaching program effectiveness and ROI
- Facilitating coaching conversations and debriefs in HR contexts

## Key prompts

### Program design

1. "Design a 6-month mentoring program for [employee segment] with matching criteria."
2. "Create a peer coaching program structure for [team/function]."
3. "Build a reverse mentoring program connecting [senior leaders] with [early career employees]."
4. "Develop a group coaching program curriculum for [first-time managers]."
5. "Design a coaching culture roadmap for an organization at [maturity level]."

### Coaching frameworks and conversations

1. "Walk me through using the GROW model for a coaching conversation about [situation]."
2. "Create a coaching conversation guide for a manager addressing [performance/development issue]."
3. "Write powerful coaching questions for exploring [career transition/leadership challenge]."
4. "Develop a coaching session structure for a 60-minute conversation."
5. "Create a coaching debrief template for tracking progress across sessions."

### Manager coaching capability

1. "Design a manager coaching skills training program with practice scenarios."
2. "Create a coaching skills assessment for managers at [level]."
3. "Develop a manager guide for transitioning from directive management to coaching."
4. "Write behavioral indicators of coaching leadership for use in performance reviews."
5. "Create a coaching conversation starter toolkit for busy managers."

### Mentor enablement

1. "Write a mentor onboarding guide covering expectations, boundaries, and best practices."
2. "Create a mentee preparation toolkit for getting the most from mentoring relationships."
3. "Develop a mentor-mentee relationship charter template."
4. "Design mentor training covering active listening, feedback, and goal-setting."
5. "Write a guide for mentors navigating difficult conversations with mentees."

## Tips

- Distinguish clearly between coaching (drawing out answers) and mentoring (sharing experience)—conflating them weakens both.
- Design mentoring matches around goals and development needs, not just seniority or function.
- Build psychological safety before introducing coaching conversations—employees must trust the process is developmental, not evaluative.
- Train managers in one coaching model deeply rather than exposing them to many superficially.
- Measure mentoring relationship quality through periodic check-ins, not just program completion rates.
- Champion coaching from the top—when leaders visibly use and model coaching behaviors, adoption accelerates.
