# Coaching and mentoring prompts

Use these prompts when designing mentoring programs, building manager coaching skills, or structuring individual coaching conversations.

- "Design a 6-month structured mentoring program for [number] high-potential [individual contributors / managers] identified in the last talent review — including matching criteria, conversation frameworks, check-in cadence, and how program success is measured."
- "Walk me through using the GROW model to structure a coaching conversation with a [manager / individual contributor] who is avoiding a difficult performance conversation with a direct report."
- "Build a reverse mentoring program that connects [senior leaders] with [early-career employees] — including the matching logic, session structure, guidelines for both parties, and how to make the exchange genuinely two-directional."
- "Design a manager coaching skills training program for [number] managers at [company] — including the core coaching behaviors to teach, the practice scenarios to use, and how to assess whether managers are applying the skills on the job."
- "Create a mentor onboarding guide covering what is expected of mentors at [company], how to structure the first mentoring conversation, how to handle a mentee who disengages, and what the mentor's boundaries should be."
- "Write a set of 10 coaching questions for use in a career development conversation with a [mid-career professional / senior individual contributor] who feels stuck but hasn't defined what 'stuck' means."
- "How do we measure the outcomes of a [mentoring program / coaching initiative] beyond participation rates — and what leading indicators tell us whether the program is actually working before the 12-month review?"
- "Design a peer coaching program for [team / function] where team members coach each other on real work challenges — including how to structure sessions, how to train participants, and how to maintain boundaries."
- "Write a guide for mentors at [company] on how to navigate a difficult mentoring conversation — including when the mentee's goals conflict with the mentor's advice, when the mentee is underperforming, or when the relationship is stalling."
- "Design a group coaching cohort experience for [first-time managers / new HRBPs] at [company] covering [specific challenge area] — including session frequency, format, facilitation approach, and how to sustain behavioral change between sessions."
