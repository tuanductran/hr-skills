# Navigating country-specific M&A integration requirements

Use these prompts when planning or executing cross-border M&A workforce integration across major markets.

- "What are the mandatory employee information and consultation requirements before we can implement integration changes for our [Germany/France/Netherlands] employees?"
- "Compare the redundancy process requirements across [UK/Germany/France] for a post-merger restructuring that will eliminate 80 roles across these three markets."
- "What does the works council consultation process look like in [Germany/France/Spain], and what integration actions are blocked until consultation is complete?"
- "Create a Day 1 readiness checklist for the HR integration team covering what must be in place for employees in [US/UK/India] from the moment the deal closes."
- "What employment term changes (compensation, benefits, job title, location) trigger mandatory employee consultation or consent requirements in [EU member states]?"
- "Identify the top 5 labor law risks in our [US + Germany + India] multi-country deal that could delay the integration timeline or create legal liability."
- "How does the TUPE regulation in the UK affect how we communicate the acquisition to target company employees before deal close?"
- "What are the practical differences between how we can restructure workforces post-merger in the US versus the EU — timelines, notice requirements, severance obligations?"
