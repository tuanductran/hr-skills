# Attendance Policy Prompts

Prompts for drafting and refining time and attendance policy language.

- "Rewrite this attendance policy in plain language for hourly retail employees."
- "Create a grace-period rule for clock-ins within [X] minutes of shift start."
- "Draft an FAQ answering common employee questions about the new attendance policy."
- "Write a policy exception process for attendance issues caused by public transit disruptions."
- "Create a template for documenting an attendance-related coaching conversation."
- "Draft a policy addendum covering remote/hybrid attendance tracking expectations."
- "Summarize how attendance occurrences reset under a rolling 12-month window vs. a calendar year."
