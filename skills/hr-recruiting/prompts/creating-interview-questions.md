# Creating interview questions

This AI assistant can be used to create interview questions by generating relevant and insightful prompts based on a given topic or theme. By inputting a topic, it can generate a list of questions and prompts that can inspire and guide the interview process. It can also help with refining and tailoring the questions to a specific audience, taking into account their interests and background knowledge.

- "I'm looking to create interview questions that highlight interviewees' [specific skill or trait], while also exploring how they've used this skill/trait to [specific accomplishment or project]. Can you suggest some questions?"
- "I need help creating interview questions that delve into interviewees' perspectives on [specific topic related to industry or role], and how they've dealt with any challenges or controversies related to this topic in their work."
- "I'm hoping to create interview questions that assess interviewees' creative problem-solving abilities by asking them to [specific challenge or scenario] and brainstorm possible solutions. Can you help me with this?"
- "I need to create interview questions that assess interviewees' communication and collaboration skills by asking them to describe a time when they [specific task or project] with a team and what role they played in achieving success."
- "I'm looking to create interview questions that assess interviewees' ability to think strategically by asking them to outline a plan for [specific goal or objective], including potential challenges and how they would address them. Any suggestions?"
