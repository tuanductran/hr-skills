# Screening resumes for qualifications

This AI assistant can be an excellent tool for screening resumes for qualifications. With its natural language processing abilities, it can quickly analyze resumes and identify the most qualified candidates for the job. By using the assistant, you can save time and reduce bias in the hiring process. Here are some tips and use cases to get the most out of the assistant for this task.

- "Can you provide a list of the top 5 candidates who have the most relevant experience in {industry}?"
- "Please list the candidates who have a degree in {field} and have worked in {industry} for more than {number} years."
- "Which candidates have skills in {skill} and have worked for companies with over {number} employees?"
- "Can you identify candidates who have experience with {software/technology} and have worked on projects related to {field}?"
- "Who are the top candidates who have a certification in {field} and have worked in {industry} for over {number} years?"
