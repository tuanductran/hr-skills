# Knowledge management prompts

Use these prompts when building internal HR knowledge bases, organizing employee wikis, capturing institutional knowledge, and setting documentation standards.

- "Draft an internal HR knowledge base taxonomy and folder structure for organizing employee policies, benefits guides, and manager SOPs."
- "Create an HR documentation style guide establishing tone, formatting rules, version control standards, and review schedules for policy documents."
- "Write a knowledge transfer playbook for departing key employees to capture operational processes, critical contacts, and project handovers."
- "Draft a manager onboarding wiki template containing essential links, approval workflows, and step-by-step administrative procedures."
- "Create an AI search optimization guide for structuring internal HR policies so AI agents and chatbots can retrieve accurate answers."
- "Draft a knowledge management health audit checklist to identify outdated wiki pages, broken links, and documentation coverage gaps."
