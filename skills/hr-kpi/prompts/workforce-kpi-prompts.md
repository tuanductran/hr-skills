# Workforce KPI prompts

Use these prompts when establishing workforce metrics, designing HR executive dashboards, tracking talent analytics, and measuring human capital ROI.

- "Draft an executive HR dashboard metric specification detailing definitions, calculation formulas, data sources, and benchmarks for key talent metrics."
- "Create a turnover analysis prompt asking an AI assistant to categorize voluntary versus involuntary attrition data by tenure, department, and manager."
- "Write a recruiting efficiency KPI report framework tracking time-to-fill, cost-per-hire, offer acceptance rate, and candidate conversion rates."
- "Draft a manager scorecard template measuring team retention, performance distribution, training completion rates, and eNPS scores."
- "Create a workforce productivity metric guide defining how to measure revenue per employee, human capital ROI, and labor cost ratios."
- "Draft a data governance guideline for HR reporting ensuring metrics consistency, privacy protection, and access control across analytics dashboards."
