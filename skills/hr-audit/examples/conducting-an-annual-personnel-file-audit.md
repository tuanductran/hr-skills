# Conducting an Annual Personnel File Audit

## Context

An HR compliance team is conducting its annual audit of personnel files across a 500-employee company to catch documentation gaps before a potential regulatory inspection.

## Step 1: Define scope and sampling

Sample prompt: "Design a sampling methodology to audit personnel files for a workforce of 500."

Expected response: A stratified random sample (e.g., 15% of files, proportionally represented across departments and locations) sized to give statistically defensible coverage without a full manual review.

## Step 2: Build the audit checklist

Sample prompt: "Create a checklist for auditing personnel files for required documentation completeness."

Expected response: A checklist covering required items (signed offer letter, eligibility-to-work documentation, signed policy acknowledgments, performance review history, up-to-date emergency contacts) with a pass/fail column per file.

## Step 3: Report and remediate

Sample prompt: "Draft an audit findings report template summarizing gaps and risk levels" and "Create a corrective action plan template for addressing an identified compliance gap."

Expected response: A findings report grouping gaps by risk level (e.g., missing eligibility documentation flagged as high risk), paired with a corrective action plan assigning an owner and 30-day deadline to each finding.

## Workflow summary

The team defines a defensible sample, audits against a clear checklist, and turns findings into a prioritized, owned corrective action plan with a scheduled follow-up review.
