# Employee experience prompts

Use these prompts when designing workplace journeys, conducting engagement surveys, improving retention, and optimizing employee touchpoints.

- "Draft an end-to-end employee lifecycle mapping framework identifying critical moments of truth from candidate outreach to offboarding exit."
- "Create an employee engagement survey action planning template for managers to discuss team survey results and co-create improvement steps."
- "Write a pulse survey questionnaire targeting onboarding sentiment, manager feedback quality, and workplace enablement."
- "Draft a stay interview conversation guide for managers to identify retention risks and career growth opportunities among top performers."
- "Create an internal communications strategy template for announcing workplace flexibility policies and employee wellbeing benefits."
- "Draft an offboarding exit survey guide focused on gathering actionable feedback on culture, compensation, and management effectiveness."
