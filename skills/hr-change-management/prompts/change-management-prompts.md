# Change management prompts

Use these prompts when leading organizational transformations, system rollouts, operational shifts, and stakeholder engagement plans.

- "Draft a change management strategy plan for transitioning an organization from legacy software to a modern cloud-based system."
- "Create a stakeholder matrix and communication plan for introducing a new hybrid work policy across global offices."
- "Write a leadership alignment workshop outline to help executive sponsors align on vision and key messages for an upcoming reorganization."
- "Draft a change readiness survey to measure employee sentiment, potential resistance, and training needs prior to system migration."
- "Create a resistance mitigation guide for managers facing pushback during a shift to centralized shared services."
- "Draft a project post-mortem template to evaluate change adoption rates, communication effectiveness, and lessons learned."
