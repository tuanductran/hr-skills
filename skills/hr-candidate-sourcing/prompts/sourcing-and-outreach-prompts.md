# Sourcing and Outreach Prompts

Use these prompts when actively searching for candidates or drafting outreach content.

- "Write a Boolean string to find [role] candidates who know [tool/skill] and have worked at [type of company]."
- "Create three variations of a first-touch outreach message for a [role], each with a different opening hook."
- "Draft a message inviting a candidate to a talent community/pipeline for future [role] openings."
- "Write a short message to send when a candidate accepts a connection request but hasn't replied to the outreach."
- "Suggest search keywords and synonyms recruiters commonly miss when sourcing for [role]."
- "Create a candidate scorecard template to evaluate sourced profiles against [role] requirements."
- "Draft a sourcing brief summarizing target companies, titles, and locations for [role]."
- "Write an outreach message that references a candidate's recent public post or project."
