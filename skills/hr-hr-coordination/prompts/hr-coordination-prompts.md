# HR coordination prompts

Use these prompts when managing day-to-day HR operations, coordinating cross-functional HR activities, or building HR process infrastructure.

- "Build a quarterly HR operations calendar for [company size] that aligns recruiting cycles, performance review windows, benefits open enrollment, compliance deadlines, and manager training into a single shared view."
- "Design an onboarding coordination checklist for [role] that spans IT, Facilities, Payroll, and HR — including who owns each step, the completion deadline, and the escalation path if a step is missed."
- "Write a RACI for the HR coordinator function at [company] covering interview scheduling, onboarding logistics, offboarding processing, benefits administration, and compliance tracking."
- "How do we build a shared HR coordination system that gives every HR team member visibility into open tasks, upcoming deadlines, and completion status without relying on email threads?"
- "Design a vendor coordination protocol for managing [benefits broker / HRIS vendor / background check provider] relationships — including who owns each relationship, how issues are escalated, and what SLAs are tracked."
- "Build a template for documenting any HR process so a coordinator can follow it without institutional knowledge — including trigger, steps, owner, tools used, and escalation path."
- "Design an HR audit preparation checklist for [annual / SOC 2 / I-9 / benefits compliance] audit that the HR coordinator can execute independently with minimal HRBP involvement."
- "Write a weekly HR operations status report template that surfaces open items, upcoming deadlines, blockers, and completed tasks for the [HR director / VP HR] in under five minutes of reading."
- "How do we coordinate a company-wide program rollout — such as [performance reviews / benefits open enrollment / engagement survey] — across [number] locations with different time zones and HR contacts?"
- "Design an offboarding coordination checklist for [involuntary / voluntary] separations covering all HR, IT, Payroll, and Facilities steps with completion ownership and timing requirements."
