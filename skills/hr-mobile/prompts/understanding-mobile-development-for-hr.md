# Understanding mobile development for HR and recruiters

Use these prompts when you need to understand mobile engineering concepts well enough to screen candidates, evaluate portfolios, or advise hiring managers.

- "Explain [React Native/Flutter/Kotlin Multiplatform] in plain terms so I can describe it accurately to candidates and hiring managers."
- "What is the difference between [native mobile development and cross-platform development], and when does a company choose one over the other?"
- "A candidate said they use [WatermelonDB/Realm/SQLite] for offline storage. Is that a strong signal for a senior mobile role, and what follow-up question should I ask?"
- "What does [mobile CI/CD/Fastlane/TestFlight/Firebase App Distribution] mean, and why does it matter for evaluating a senior mobile candidate?"
- "Explain [mobile app architecture patterns like MVVM, MVI, or Clean Architecture] in simple terms — what does it tell me if a candidate mentions them?"
- "What does it mean when a mobile developer says they have [App Store/Google Play deployment experience], and why is it a more meaningful signal than just writing code?"
- "What are the most important mobile engineering terms a technical recruiter should know when building a sourcing pipeline for [iOS/Android/React Native] roles?"
- "A hiring manager asked me to find someone who knows [Xcode Instruments/Android Profiler/Flipper]. What are these tools and why are they relevant to mobile seniority?"
