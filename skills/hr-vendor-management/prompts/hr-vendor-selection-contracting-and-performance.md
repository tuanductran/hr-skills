# HR vendor selection, contracting, and performance management

Prompts for running RFP processes, building evaluation scorecards, negotiating contracts, managing ongoing vendor performance, and planning vendor transitions.

## Needs definition and market scan

- "Help me write a vendor requirements document for [HR tool or service category]. Include: use case, target users, must-have functionality, integration requirements, and success criteria at 12 months."
- "Build a longlist of vendors for [HRIS / ATS / LMS / benefits administration / payroll / background screening] for a [company size and industry] company. Include key differentiators for each."
- "What questions should we ask current users of [vendor] before adding them to our evaluation shortlist?"
- "We are evaluating vendors for [category]. What are the most common buyer regrets or implementation failure modes we should design our evaluation to avoid?"

## RFP and evaluation

- "Write an RFP for [HR tool or service category] covering: company context, functional requirements (must-have vs. nice-to-have), integration requirements, security requirements, implementation model, and pricing structure."
- "Build a vendor evaluation scorecard for [category] comparing [Vendor A], [Vendor B], and [Vendor C]. Weight criteria by: functional fit, integration, vendor stability, security, and total cost of ownership."
- "Design a vendor demo script for [category] that tests the specific scenarios our team needs rather than letting vendors show only their best features."
- "What data security and compliance questions must we ask any vendor that will process employee or candidate personal data? Include: SOC 2, data residency, subprocessors, breach notification, and data deletion."
- "Score these three vendors against our evaluation criteria and recommend a selection with rationale: [paste scorecard results]."
- "How should we weigh a lower-cost vendor with a weaker implementation track record against a higher-cost vendor with stronger references and support for [use case]?"

## Contract negotiation

- "What contract terms should we prioritize when negotiating with [HRIS / ATS / benefits / payroll] vendors? Include: pricing, SLAs, data rights, exit provisions, and auto-renewal terms."
- "Draft negotiation talking points for our renewal discussion with [vendor] given [we have used [X]% of contracted seats / have had [Y] SLA breaches / have a competing proposal from [alternative vendor]]."
- "What SLA commitments should we require for [payroll / benefits / core HRIS] systems given the operational risk of downtime or data errors?"
- "Write the data portability and deletion clause we should insist on inserting before signing with [vendor category]."
- "What exit provisions should we negotiate before signing a [3-year / multi-year] contract with [vendor] to ensure we are not locked in if the vendor underperforms?"
- "Benchmark this pricing proposal from [vendor] against typical market rates for [category] at [our employee count]. Is this fair?"
- "The vendor is pushing for auto-renewal with a 30-day opt-out window. Draft our counter-proposal for a 90-day window with a rate cap on renewal pricing."

## Vendor onboarding and implementation

- "Design an onboarding checklist for a new [HRIS / benefits / payroll] vendor covering: kickoff, integration setup, user acceptance testing, training, and go-live sign-off."
- "What should be defined and agreed in writing before the implementation of [vendor] begins, to prevent scope creep and go-live delays?"
- "Write the go-live success criteria for our [vendor] implementation so both parties agree on what a successful launch looks like."
- "Design a 30-day and 90-day post-go-live review agenda for our new [vendor] relationship."

## Ongoing performance management

- "Build a vendor performance scorecard for [vendor] with metrics covering: uptime, support response time, issue resolution, and user satisfaction. Include targets and a quarterly review template."
- "Design a Quarterly Business Review agenda for our [HRIS / ATS / payroll] vendor covering: SLA review, open issues, roadmap update, relationship health, and action items."
- "Write a formal vendor performance notice letter for [vendor] documenting [specific SLA breach or service failure] and requesting a remediation plan within [timeframe]."
- "What early warning signs suggest a vendor relationship is deteriorating before it reaches a formal escalation or exit decision?"
- "Design a vendor governance framework for our HR tech stack including: central vendor registry, ownership model, review cadence, and escalation path."

## Consolidation and stack rationalization

- "Audit our current HR vendor stack for redundancy and consolidation opportunities. We currently use: [list vendors and their primary functions]."
- "We have [number] vendors performing overlapping functions in [category]. Build a consolidation business case covering: cost savings, complexity reduction, and risks of consolidation."
- "Which HR vendor categories typically offer the highest ROI from consolidation at [our company size]?"

## Vendor transition and exit

- "Plan a vendor transition from [current vendor] to [new vendor] for [category]. Cover: data migration, parallel-run period, employee communication, and cutover timeline."
- "What data must we extract from [outgoing vendor] before terminating the contract, and what format should we require for portability?"
- "Write an employee communication announcing the transition from [current benefits / payroll / HR system] to [new vendor], covering: what is changing, when, and what employees need to do."
- "Design a vendor exit risk assessment for switching from [current vendor], including: data loss risk, integration disruption, employee impact, and timeline risk."
