# Job architecture prompts

Use these prompts when establishing job families, leveling frameworks, career pathways, and job classification structures across an organization.

- "Draft a job architecture leveling guide defining scope, complexity, autonomy, and impact criteria across IC1 through IC6 software engineering levels."
- "Create a job family catalog framework outlining core responsibilities, titling conventions, and skill requirements for [department name] roles."
- "Write a career dual-track framework mapping equivalencies between individual contributor pathways and management leadership pathways."
- "Draft a manager communication toolkit for introducing updated job levels, compensation bands, and career progressions to employees."
- "Create a job evaluation methodology guide for assessing role complexity and assigning appropriate salary bands to newly created positions."
- "Draft a job architecture audit protocol to identify non-standard job titles, level inflation, and internal equity discrepancies across business units."
