# Social recruiting prompts

Use these prompts when launching social media hiring campaigns, building candidate talent pools on professional platforms, and driving employee advocacy on LinkedIn.

- "Draft LinkedIn post copy announcing open software engineering roles with engaging visual hooks, team highlights, and clear call-to-action links."
- "Create a social media recruiting campaign calendar for a 4-week talent brand campaign highlighting company culture and remote work perks."
- "Write an employee advocacy toolkit guiding team members on how to share open jobs and company news on their personal social channels."
- "Draft a candidate outreach strategy guide for engaging passive talent in specialized developer communities on Discord, Reddit, and GitHub."
- "Create a Zalo recruiting campaign strategy template for reaching tech candidates in Vietnam, including message templates and group posting guidelines."
- "Draft a social recruiting performance report tracking click-through rates, applicant conversion, and applicant source quality across social platforms."
