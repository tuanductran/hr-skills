# HR business partnering prompts

Use these prompts when advising business leaders, building strategic workforce plans, coaching managers, and driving organizational alignment.

- "Draft a strategic workforce planning agenda for a quarterly business review with executive department leaders in [department name]."
- "Create a manager coaching framework for guiding department heads through difficult performance management conversations."
- "Write an organizational diagnostic survey prompt to identify operational friction between engineering and product teams."
- "Draft a talent review calibration preparation guide for business leaders to evaluate high-potential employees and succession risks."
- "Create a change impact assessment template for communicating executive restructuring plans to affected business units."
- "Draft a business case template for HR business partners proposing new headcount allocations based on revenue and workload projections."
