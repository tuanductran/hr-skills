# Product Management Hiring Prompts

- "Design a product-sense case study asking a candidate to redesign the onboarding flow for [product type]."
- "Draft follow-up questions to probe how a candidate would measure success for their proposed solution."
- "Create a rubric scoring a candidate's ability to prioritize under resource constraints."
- "Draft behavioral questions asking how a candidate handled disagreement with an engineering lead."
- "Write a take-home exercise asking a candidate to build a 2-quarter roadmap given competing stakeholder requests."
- "Draft reference-check questions asking specifically about measurable outcomes from a candidate's past launch."
