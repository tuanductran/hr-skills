# Building a Visa Expiration Tracking Process

## Context

A growing tech company has 25 employees on employer-sponsored visas but no centralized system for tracking renewal deadlines, and a recent near-miss expiration prompted leadership to fix the gap.

## Step 1: Design the tracking process

Sample prompt: "Design a process for tracking visa expiration dates and renewal deadlines."

Expected response: A centralized tracker capturing visa type, expiration date, renewal lead time required, and responsible owner, with automated alerts at 180, 90, and 30 days before expiration.

## Step 2: Build the compliance calendar

Sample prompt: "Design a compliance calendar tracking immigration-related deadlines across the workforce."

Expected response: A shared calendar consolidating all upcoming deadlines across the sponsored population, reviewed monthly by HR and shared with immigration counsel.

## Step 3: Draft communication

Sample prompt: "Draft a reminder email template to send 90 days before a visa expiration date."

Expected response: A clear, reassuring email to the employee explaining the renewal timeline, what documentation they need to provide, and who to contact with questions.

## Workflow summary

HR builds a centralized tracker with automated lead-time alerts, consolidates deadlines into a shared compliance calendar, and proactively communicates with employees well before expiration risk becomes urgent.
