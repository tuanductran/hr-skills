# Skills-based talent practices

Prompts for applying skills data to hiring, internal mobility, learning, succession, and workforce planning decisions.

## Skills-based hiring

- "Redesign the job requirements for [role] using skills-based criteria instead of degree and title requirements. Identify the five to seven skills that genuinely predict success."
- "Write a skills-based job description for [role] that removes unnecessary credential requirements and focuses on demonstrated capabilities."
- "Design a skills assessment process for hiring [role] that evaluates [skill list] without relying on resume screening alone."
- "Create structured interview questions that assess [skill] at [proficiency level] for a [role] candidate, including what a strong versus weak answer looks like."
- "Build a candidate skills scorecard for [role] with defined proficiency thresholds and a scoring guide for interviewers."
- "Review this job description and identify credential or experience requirements that could be replaced with skills-based criteria: [paste job description]."

## Internal mobility and career pathways

- "Map the skills adjacencies between [current role] and [target role] to identify which employees are strong internal candidates with minimal reskilling."
- "Design an internal skills marketplace framework for [organization size] that connects employees to project work based on skills rather than job titles."
- "Create a skills-based career pathway for [role family] showing how employees can progress laterally or vertically based on skills development rather than tenure."
- "Identify the top [number] internal mobility opportunities for employees with strong [skill] based on our current open roles and skills inventory."
- "Write a skills-based internal job posting template that emphasizes required skills and proficiency levels over credentials and years of experience."

## Learning pathway design

- "Map the skills gaps in [function] to specific learning interventions — courses, coaching, mentoring, and on-the-job assignments — with a timeline and owner for each."
- "Design a learning pathway for transitioning [current role] employees into [emerging role], covering required skills, development sequence, and estimated timeline."
- "Create a skills development plan template for employees and managers that links individual goals to priority skills gaps identified in the last gap analysis."
- "Build a critical skills acceleration program for [high-priority skill] covering: target audience, learning format, proficiency milestones, and success metrics."
- "Design a skills progress tracking framework that measures whether learning interventions are moving employee proficiency scores over a [timeframe]."

## Succession planning integration

- "Assess the readiness of [successor name or role] for [target role] based on the following skills profile comparison: [paste current vs. required skills]."
- "Identify skills development priorities for our top [number] succession candidates preparing for [leadership level] roles over the next [timeframe]."
- "Design a skills-based succession readiness review process that evaluates candidates against role-specific skills requirements, not just performance ratings."
- "Build a succession risk report showing which critical roles have no internal successor with the required skills profile."

## Workforce planning alignment

- "Develop a skills demand forecast for [function] aligned to [strategic initiative], identifying which skills will be needed more, less, or that are currently absent."
- "Classify our current skills inventory into build (develop internally), buy (hire externally), and borrow (contingent or partnered) categories based on scarcity and criticality."
- "Identify key person risk in our [function or team] — skills held by a small number of individuals with no internal backup or succession plan."
- "Design a skills-based workforce segmentation for [organization] that groups employees by skills profile rather than job title for planning purposes."

## Skills governance and maintenance

- "Design a skills taxonomy governance process including: ownership, review cadence, contribution rules, and how to retire obsolete skills."
- "Write a skills data policy covering: what skills data is collected, how it is used in talent decisions, employee access rights, and retention periods."
- "Create an annual skills taxonomy review checklist covering: emerging skills to add, obsolete skills to retire, proficiency anchors to update, and benchmark comparison."
- "Design an employee communication plan for launching a new skills inventory system, covering: purpose, timeline, what employees need to do, and how data will be used."
