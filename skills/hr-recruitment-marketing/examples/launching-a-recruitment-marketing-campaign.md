# Launching a recruitment marketing campaign

## Context

A company needs to launch a targeted employer brand campaign to attract specialized DevOps and Cloud engineers in a competitive hiring market.

## Step 1: Define EVP messaging and audience pillars

Sample prompt: "Draft key messaging pillars for a recruitment marketing campaign targeting Cloud and DevOps Engineers."

Expected response: Three core messaging pillars focusing on technical autonomy (modern cloud stack), career growth (continuous learning stipend), and work-life balance (flexible work).

## Step 2: Build campaign content assets and copy

Sample prompt: "Create ad copy variations and employee spotlight prompts for LinkedIn and developer community platforms."

Expected response: Multi-channel content copy tailored for LinkedIn feed posts, developer platform banner ads, and an employee video spotlight script.

## Step 3: Monitor campaign performance and conversion

Sample prompt: "Draft a recruitment marketing campaign ROI dashboard tracking channel click-through rates, landing page conversions, and cost-per-hire."

Expected response: A analytics reporting layout measuring channel performance, applicant quality rates, and total campaign ROI across digital channels.

## Workflow summary

The recruitment marketing manager defines core EVP pillars, produces targeted digital ad content across developer channels, and measures conversion metrics to optimize hiring attraction.
