# Recruitment marketing prompts

Use these prompts when planning employer brand campaigns, creating recruitment ad copy, building career page content, and measuring candidate attraction metrics.

- "Draft employer value proposition (EVP) messaging frameworks for targeting technical engineering candidates."
- "Create recruitment marketing ad copy variations for social media campaigns highlighting company growth, engineering culture, and benefits."
- "Write career page copy highlighting team mission, diversity commitments, career development opportunities, and employee testimonials."
- "Draft an employer branding content calendar featuring employee spotlights, tech blog highlights, and office culture videos."
- "Create a recruitment marketing campaign briefing template outlining campaign goals, target audience, budget, and channel distribution."
- "Draft a candidate acquisition channel performance dashboard specification tracking campaign impressions, clicks, applications, and cost-per-applicant."
