# HR policy management prompts

Use these prompts when drafting, reviewing, communicating, or governing HR policies and employee handbooks.

- "Write a [remote work / flexible work / hybrid work] policy for a [company size/industry] organization, covering eligibility, expectations, equipment, and performance accountability."
- "Draft a performance improvement policy with a clear process from informal coaching through formal PIP to potential termination, including documentation requirements at each stage."
- "Create a disciplinary action policy covering verbal warning, written warning, final warning, and termination — with guidance on when each step can be skipped."
- "Review this existing [policy name] for gaps, outdated language, and inconsistency with current employment law — flag every issue and recommend updates."
- "Design a policy review and approval workflow for [company governance structure] — who drafts, who reviews, who approves, and how frequently."
- "Create a policy register template to track all HR policies by name, owner, last review date, next review date, and status."
- "Write manager talking points for explaining the [updated remote work / disciplinary / leave] policy to their teams, including common questions and how to answer them."
- "Develop a policy acknowledgment workflow for digital signature and tracking — including how to follow up with employees who haven't acknowledged."
- "Write a change summary document comparing old vs. new [policy] for employees — one page, plain language, highlighting what changed and why."
- "Build a policy audit checklist to identify gaps, overlapping policies, and policies that haven't been reviewed in over 24 months."
