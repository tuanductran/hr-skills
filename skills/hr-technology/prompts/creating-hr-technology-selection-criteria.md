# Creating HR technology selection criteria

This AI assistant can assist in creating HR technology selection criteria by providing insights on HR technologies, identifying key features and capabilities of each technology, and suggesting relevant criteria to evaluate them. With its vast knowledge and language generation capabilities, it can help to streamline the selection process and ensure that the most suitable technology is selected for an organization's HR needs.

- "What are some [specific HR technology] available for [recruitment/employee engagement/performance management], and how do they [specific feature or capability]?"
- "Can you suggest key features and capabilities to look for when evaluating HR technologies for [specific HR need], and how do they [specific benefit]?"
- "How can HR technology improve [specific HR goal, e.g. diversity and inclusion], and what [specific features or metrics] should I look for in a technology to support these goals?"
- "What are some important criteria to consider when selecting HR technology for [small/medium/large] organizations, and how do they [specific impact on organization]?"
- "Can you provide a comparison of different HR technologies for [specific HR need], including [specific criteria or features to compare], and what are the [specific strengths and weaknesses] of each?"
