# Developing HR technology vendor evaluations

This AI assistant can be a useful tool for developing HR technology vendor evaluations. By using prompts to guide the conversation, it can help you generate questions and criteria to evaluate vendors, and provide you with a wealth of information to help you make informed decisions.

- "Can you provide me with an overview of the ways in [HR technology], and how they are impacting [vendor evaluations] in [specific industry or context]?"
- "What are some key criteria to consider when evaluating [HR technology] vendors, and how do they vary by [industry/ company size/ specific needs]?"
- "Which [HR technology] vendors are currently leading the market, and what are their key strengths and weaknesses, particularly in the areas of [specific feature or service] and [specific industry or context]?"
- "What are some best practices for conducting effective [vendor evaluations], and how can we ensure that we are getting accurate and unbiased information from [internal/external stakeholders]?"
- "How can we use data and analytics to inform our [HR technology] vendor evaluations, and what metrics should we be tracking, particularly in the areas of [specific business outcome or KPI] and [specific industry or context]?"
