# HR technology

Comprehensive HR technology support — from evaluating and selecting HR systems to implementing HRIS, building HR technology roadmaps, managing automation, and developing digital HR solutions.

## Supported tasks

- Creating HR technology roadmaps and selection criteria
- Evaluating and selecting HR technology vendors
- Implementing HR systems and managing projects
- Conducting HR cloud migration projects
- Developing HR chatbot and mobile application solutions
- Creating HR self-service portals and data governance frameworks
- Managing HR automation and data integration projects
- Writing HR technology RFPs and business cases
- Developing HR gamification platforms
- Managing HR technology vendors and data visualization platforms

## Key prompts

### Technology strategy and roadmaps

1. "Help me create an HR technology roadmap for [organization] for the next 3 years, prioritizing [key capabilities]."
2. "What should an HR technology strategy include to support our people goals and business objectives?"
3. "How should I develop HR technology selection criteria for evaluating [HRIS/ATS/LMS/payroll] vendors?"
4. "What are the key trends in HR technology that [company size/industry] should prioritize in [current year]?"
5. "How can I create an HR technology business case for investing in [specific system/capability]?"

### System selection and RFPs

1. "Write an HR technology RFP for [system type, for example, HRIS, ATS, performance management tool] for [company size]."
2. "What evaluation criteria should I use when selecting an [ATS/HRIS/LMS] for [organization profile]?"
3. "How should I structure a vendor evaluation process for selecting a new [HR system]?"
4. "What questions should I ask vendors during demos for [system type]?"
5. "How should I conduct an HR technology vendor evaluation that includes both technical and user experience criteria?"

### Implementation and migration

1. "What are the key steps to successfully implement a new [HRIS/ATS/performance management system]?"
2. "How should I plan and manage an HR cloud migration project to minimize disruption?"
3. "What change management strategies help employees adopt a new HR system successfully?"
4. "How can I create an HR data migration plan that ensures data integrity during system transitions?"
5. "What are the most common HR system implementation failures and how can I avoid them?"

### HR automation and integration

1. "What HR processes are best suited for automation, and how should I prioritize which to automate first?"
2. "How can I manage HR automation initiatives that reduce administrative burden without sacrificing employee experience?"
3. "What should an HR data integration project plan include to connect [system A] with [system B]?"
4. "How can I build an HR self-service portal that empowers employees and managers to handle common requests?"

### Digital HR solutions

1. "How should we develop an HR chatbot solution to handle common employee questions about [benefits/policies/HR processes]?"
2. "What are best practices for developing mobile HR applications that improve the employee experience?"
3. "How can HR gamification platforms be used to improve adoption of [training/wellness/performance] programs?"
4. "What social media strategies can HR use to strengthen employer brand and support recruiting?"

### Data governance and security

1. "What should an HR data governance framework include to manage employee data responsibly?"
2. "How can I create HR data warehousing solutions that support analytics and reporting needs?"
3. "Write an HR cybersecurity policy that protects sensitive employee data from breaches and unauthorized access."
4. "What are the key components of an HR data privacy policy that complies with GDPR and other regulations?"
5. "How should I manage HR data visualization platforms to ensure data accuracy and access security?"

### ROI and reporting

1. "How can I create an HR technology ROI report that demonstrates the value of our [system] investment?"
2. "Write HR data analytics reports that show the business impact of our HR technology implementations."
3. "What metrics should I track to evaluate the success of an HR system implementation?"

## Tips

- Define business requirements and user stories before evaluating vendors — don't let vendors define your needs.
- Involve end users (HR team, managers, employees) in system selection to ensure adoption.
- Plan for change management from day one — technology implementation is 20% technical, 80% people.
- Build data governance before deploying analytics tools — clean, consistent data is prerequisite to insight.
- Calculate total cost of ownership (license, implementation, training, maintenance) when comparing vendors.
