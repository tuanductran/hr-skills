# Building a 24/7 Support Team Rotation

## Context

A SaaS company is scaling its customer support team to provide 24/7 coverage across three time zones with a team of 12 agents.

## Step 1: Forecast demand

Sample prompt: "Forecast staffing needs for a support team based on ticket volume by hour."

Expected response: A breakdown of expected ticket volume by hour block, translated into required concurrent agents per block using average handle time.

## Step 2: Design the rotation

Sample prompt: "Design a rotating shift pattern for a 24/7 operation with 3 teams."

Expected response: A rotation pattern (e.g., three 8-hour shifts per day, teams rotating through day/evening/night blocks on a set cycle) with built-in rest periods between rotations.

## Step 3: Draft the swap policy

Sample prompt: "Write a shift swap policy including approval steps and deadlines."

Expected response: A policy requiring swaps to be requested at least 48 hours in advance, approved by a team lead, and logged in the scheduling system to preserve coverage visibility.

## Workflow summary

The team forecasts demand by hour, builds a rotation that provides continuous coverage while respecting rest periods, and puts a transparent swap policy in place to handle day-to-day changes without gaps.
