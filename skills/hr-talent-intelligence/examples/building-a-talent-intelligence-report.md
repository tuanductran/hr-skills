# Building a talent intelligence report

## Context

An executive leadership team plans to expand engineering operations into a new regional hub and needs market talent intelligence to evaluate location choices and talent availability.

## Step 1: Analyze talent supply and compensation benchmarks

Sample prompt: "Draft a labor market supply analysis comparing tech talent availability, salary benchmarks, and university pipelines in target expansion cities."

Expected response: A comparative matrix evaluating talent pool size, average compensation bands, university graduate counts, and tech ecosystem maturity across candidate cities.

## Step 2: Conduct competitor landscape analysis

Sample prompt: "Create a competitor talent footprint breakdown analyzing key employers, hiring velocity, and remote work policies."

Expected response: A competitor analysis detailing major employers in the market, typical talent poached from competitors, and regional salary premiums.

## Step 3: Executive synthesis and location recommendation

Sample prompt: "Draft an executive talent intelligence briefing recommending an expansion city based on cost, quality, and hiring speed."

Expected response: A strategic decision memo summarizing talent availability, projected time-to-fill, compensation recommendations, and risk mitigations for executive approval.

## Workflow summary

The talent analyst gathers market data, benchmark salaries, and competitor intelligence to produce an actionable location strategy report for executive leaders.
