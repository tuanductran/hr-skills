# Talent intelligence prompts

Use these prompts when researching labor market trends, analyzing competitor talent movements, evaluating talent availability, and mapping compensation benchmarks.

- "Draft a market availability report prompt analyzing talent supply and demand for [role title] in [location/region]."
- "Create a competitive talent intelligence framework comparing target company headcount, hiring velocity, and skill hubs."
- "Write a talent migration analysis outline tracking candidate movements between key competitor companies."
- "Draft executive talking points summarizing labor market data, compensation inflation, and remote work trends for workforce planning."
- "Create a skill adjacency mapping guide identifying transferable skill sets for hard-to-fill technical positions."
- "Draft a talent intelligence data collection protocol covering public data sources, salary surveys, and industry reports."
