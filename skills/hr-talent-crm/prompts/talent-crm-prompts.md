# Talent CRM prompts

Use these prompts when building candidate talent pools, setting up candidate nurture workflows, segmenting talent communities, and optimizing candidate engagement in CRM platforms.

- "Draft a candidate nurture email sequence for passive silver-medalist candidates who reached final interview rounds."
- "Create a talent CRM segmentation taxonomy for categorizing candidate pipelines by domain skill, seniority level, and geographic preference."
- "Write a quarterly talent community newsletter template sharing company engineering blogs, product milestones, and open roles."
- "Draft candidate re-engagement campaign prompts for re-activating dormant candidate profiles in the CRM."
- "Create a talent CRM hygiene checklist for auditing data accuracy, tag consistency, and consent status across candidate pools."
- "Draft a talent CRM integration specification for syncing candidate data between ATS sourcing channels and nurture campaigns."
