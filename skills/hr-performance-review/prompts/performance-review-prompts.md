# Performance review prompts

Use these prompts when running performance evaluation cycles, drafting self-assessments, writing manager reviews, and conducting 360-degree feedback loops.

- "Draft a manager guide for writing objective, evidence-based performance review summaries that focus on impact rather than personality traits."
- "Create a 360-degree peer feedback survey template containing open-ended prompts and competency rating scales for software engineers."
- "Write an employee self-assessment preparation worksheet guiding staff on how to highlight key achievements, growth areas, and goal alignment."
- "Draft a performance calibration meeting guide for department leaders to align ratings, prevent grade inflation, and ensure fairness across teams."
- "Create a performance improvement plan template with SMART goals, weekly check-in schedules, and clear success criteria."
- "Draft a post-review conversation script for managers delivering constructive feedback and setting goals for the upcoming performance period."
