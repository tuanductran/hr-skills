# Market mapping prompts

Use these prompts when building talent market maps, sizing talent pools, or preparing competitive intelligence for a search.

- "Build a talent market map for [role/function] across [industry/region], identifying 15-20 target companies and explaining why each is a good source."
- "Segment the [role] talent market by seniority level, specialization, and geography within [industry] to help prioritize sourcing effort."
- "Identify which companies we should exclude from sourcing for [role] due to non-compete restrictions, prior relationships, or reputational concerns."
- "Map the reporting structure for [function] at [target company] based on publicly available information — org chart, LinkedIn, and job postings."
- "Compare the talent value propositions of [company A] and [company B] for [role] — compensation positioning, brand strength, career growth signals, and recent attrition indicators."
- "Estimate the addressable talent market size for [role] with [specific skill or certification] in [region]."
- "Identify feeder companies and career paths that typically produce strong [role] candidates ready for a step-up opportunity."
- "Summarize this market map into a one-page briefing for the hiring manager, explaining what it means for timeline, comp positioning, and outreach strategy."
- "Identify recent leadership changes or reorganizations at [target company] that could open sourcing opportunities in the next 90 days."
- "Draft an executive summary of market mapping findings for a [function] search, including scarcity signals, compensation benchmarks, and recommended sourcing priorities."
