# Applying competencies across talent processes

Use these prompts when embedding competencies into hiring, performance, development, or succession processes.

- "Design a competency-based interview guide for [role] that maps 4-5 interview questions to specific competencies with behavioral scoring anchors."
- "Write structured behavioral interview questions for the competency [customer focus/collaboration/problem solving] at the [mid-level/senior/leadership] proficiency tier."
- "Create a competency-based performance review form that assesses both what was achieved and how it was achieved, using the competencies in our framework."
- "Build a development planning template that connects an employee's competency assessment gap to specific learning actions, assignments, and coaching."
- "Design a succession readiness assessment using our leadership competency framework to evaluate potential successors against the requirements of [target role]."
- "How should we weight competency ratings versus performance outcomes in our annual review process to avoid halo effects?"
- "Create a calibration guide that helps managers apply behavioral indicators consistently so competency ratings mean the same thing across teams."
- "Write the talent review discussion guide that uses competency data alongside performance to make promotion and development decisions."
