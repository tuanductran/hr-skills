# Writing performance appraisal reports

This AI assistant can be used to assist with writing performance appraisal reports by providing suggestions for performance indicators, strengths and weaknesses of an employee, and specific examples of their work. It can also offer feedback on the overall structure and tone of the report. With its vast knowledge base and language capabilities, it can help you write a more detailed and personalized performance appraisal report.

- "Please provide [number] specific examples of [employee name]'s work that demonstrate their outstanding performance in [skill/competency], as well as any areas where they could improve their performance in [skill/competency]. Please also include any feedback that [employee name] received from their peers, clients, or managers regarding their performance in these areas."
- "Considering [employee name]'s role and responsibilities, what are [number] weaknesses that they should work on improving, and how can they do so? Please also provide recommendations for training, coaching, or other resources that could help [employee name] improve their skills and performance."
- "Can you suggest a more effective way to communicate [employee name]'s accomplishments in the report, highlighting their contributions to the company's success and growth? Please provide specific examples of [employee name]'s achievements and how they added value to the company, as well as any quantitative data that supports their impact."
- "How can I structure the report in a way that accurately reflects [employee name]'s performance and provides actionable feedback for their professional development, while also being clear and concise for the intended audience? Please provide a detailed outline of the report structure, including sections, headings, and subheadings, as well as tips on how to organize the content effectively."
- "Based on [employee name]'s performance this year, can you suggest [number] performance indicators to include in the report that reflect their strengths in [skill/competency] and areas for improvement in [skill/competency]? Please also provide examples of how [employee name] demonstrated each of these indicators in their work over the past year."
