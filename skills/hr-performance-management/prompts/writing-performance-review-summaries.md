# Writing performance review summaries

This AI assistant can be used to assist with writing performance review summaries. By providing relevant prompts and suggestions, it can help in creating concise, informative and personalized summaries based on the provided data. The AI can also help in identifying areas of improvement and strengths of an individual, allowing for a more thorough and accurate assessment.

- "Based on [employee's name]'s [number] key performance indicators for [specific project/period], can you generate a summary of their strengths and areas for improvement? Please also include any noteworthy accomplishments or contributions, as well as [specific metric] data to support your assessment."
- "How would you summarize [team/department name]'s performance this [quarter/semester/year], taking into account [specific event or circumstance] that impacted their work? Please highlight [number] key achievements, [number] areas for opportunity, and provide recommendations for how they can improve in the next [timeframe]."
- "What specific actions can [employee's name] take to improve in [skill or area], based on their [project/task] performance and feedback from [specific source or group]? Please provide a summary of their overall performance and rating, and outline [number] specific steps they can take to develop their skills and achieve their goals."
- "Please provide a performance review summary for [employee's name] with a focus on their [competency or skill], as it relates to their [job function or responsibilities]. Please provide [number] specific examples of their contributions to the team and the company, as well as any challenges they faced and how they overcame them."
- "Can you generate a summary of [employee's name]'s performance, highlighting their key achievements, areas for development, and how they align with the company's [core values/mission/vision]? Please provide [number] specific examples to support your summary, as well as recommendations for how they can continue to grow and make an impact in their role."
