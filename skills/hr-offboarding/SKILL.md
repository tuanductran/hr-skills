# HR offboarding

Design and execute offboarding programs that protect the organization, support departing employees, and capture institutional knowledge — covering voluntary and involuntary separations, exit insights, and alumni engagement.

## Supported tasks

- Designing end-to-end offboarding workflows for voluntary and involuntary departures
- Creating offboarding checklists for HR, managers, and IT
- Writing exit interview guides and questionnaires
- Analyzing exit interview data to identify retention risks
- Developing separation agreement templates and communication scripts
- Managing knowledge transfer processes for departing employees
- Designing alumni engagement and rehire programs
- Communicating departures to teams with sensitivity and clarity
- Building involuntary termination protocols compliant with local labor law
- Creating offboarding dashboards to track completion and trends

## Key prompts

### Offboarding process design

1. "Create a complete offboarding checklist for a [role] departing in [notice period]."
2. "Design an offboarding workflow for remote employees across [countries]."
3. "Build an offboarding timeline from resignation to last day for [company size]."
4. "Create role-specific offboarding checklists for [manager/IC/contractor]."
5. "Develop a knowledge transfer template for a departing [senior/technical] employee."

### Exit interviews

1. "Write exit interview questions to uncover the real reasons employees leave."
2. "Create a structured exit survey for [voluntary departures]."
3. "Develop an exit interview analysis framework to identify trends."
4. "Generate follow-up questions for sensitive exit interview topics."
5. "Write a script for conducting exit interviews with recently terminated employees."

### Involuntary separation

1. "Create a termination meeting script for [performance/redundancy/misconduct] separation."
2. "Develop a redundancy communication plan for [team/department] restructuring."
3. "Write a separation checklist ensuring legal compliance for [country]."
4. "Design an outplacement support package outline for [involuntary departures]."
5. "Create a manager guide for delivering difficult termination conversations."

### Alumni and rehire

1. "Design an alumni engagement program for [company/industry]."
2. "Create a boomerang employee policy and rehire process."
3. "Develop alumni communication templates for ongoing engagement."
4. "Write criteria for identifying and prioritizing rehire candidates."

## Tips

- Treat every offboarding as a brand moment—former employees become recruiters, clients, or competitors.
- Conduct exit interviews at least two weeks after the last day to get more candid feedback.
- Separate the exit interview from the HR administrative process to reduce perceived risk for the departing employee.
- Systematically analyze exit data quarterly to connect departure patterns to retention strategy.
- For involuntary separations, prepare the manager thoroughly—the conversation itself affects team morale beyond the individual.
- Build knowledge transfer into the offboarding timeline from day one of the notice period.
