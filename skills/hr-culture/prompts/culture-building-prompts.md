# Culture building prompts

Use these prompts when defining corporate values, measuring organizational culture, enhancing workplace engagement, and aligning team behaviors.

- "Draft an organizational culture assessment framework to evaluate alignment between stated company values and daily manager behaviors."
- "Create a company core values rollout communication kit including leadership talking points, team discussion guides, and recognition criteria."
- "Write an employee culture survey questionnaire targeting psychological safety, cross-team collaboration, and trust in leadership."
- "Draft a manager playbook for embedding company values into hiring interviews, onboarding welcome sessions, and performance check-ins."
- "Create an employer brand culture story framework for showcasing authentic team rituals and employee stories on social media."
- "Draft a cultural integration guide for merging teams during a merger or acquisition scenario."
