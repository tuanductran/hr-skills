# Running HR design thinking sprints

Use these prompts when facilitating design thinking workshops, design sprints, or human-centered HR problem-solving sessions.

- "Design a 3-day HR design sprint to redesign our [onboarding experience/performance review process/internal mobility program] using the five design thinking phases."
- "Create a facilitation guide for a 90-minute empathy workshop where HR and managers interview employees about their experience during [the first 90 days/a performance conversation/an internal transfer]."
- "Generate 20 'How might we...' questions for redesigning our manager effectiveness program based on employee feedback that managers are unavailable and give poor feedback."
- "Design an ideation session agenda for a cross-functional team working on reducing time-to-hire without sacrificing candidate quality."
- "Create a workshop agenda for co-designing our offboarding experience with a group of recent alumni and current employees who have been through the process."
- "Write a design thinking problem statement (point of view statement) for the challenge: employees feel the performance review process is a bureaucratic exercise with no connection to their actual development."
- "Design a rapid prototyping exercise that lets HR test three different onboarding check-in formats with a pilot group of 20 new hires before rolling out company-wide."
- "Build a design thinking retrospective to evaluate what we learned from a prototype test before deciding whether to scale, iterate, or abandon the solution."
