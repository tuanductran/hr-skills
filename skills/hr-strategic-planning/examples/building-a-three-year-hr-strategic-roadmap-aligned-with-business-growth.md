# Building a Three-Year HR Strategic Roadmap Aligned with Business Growth

## Context

A B2B SaaS enterprise planning to double ARR over the next three years needs to shift its HR function from reactive hiring to strategic business enablement. The CHRO must build a 3-year HR strategic plan aligning talent strategy with international expansion, product diversification, and operational scaling.

## Step 1: Conduct environmental scanning and capability gap analysis

Sample prompt: "Design an executive environmental scanning workshop outline to identify external talent trends, AI disrupters, and regulatory shifts."

Expected response: A workshop agenda and diagnostic framework identifying critical capability gaps in international go-to-market execution, AI engineering, and scaling leadership talent.

## Step 2: Formulate 3-year HR strategy and balanced scorecard

Sample prompt: "Build a 3-year HR strategic plan template linking strategic human capital priorities directly to enterprise growth pillars."

Expected response: A 3-year strategic roadmap articulating core pillars (e.g., Global Talent Acquisition, Engineering Capability Building, High-Performance Culture) supported by a balanced scorecard with quantifiable metrics.

## Step 3: Establish quarterly governance and resource prioritization

Sample prompt: "Draft a quarterly strategic business review (QSBR) presentation template for the CHRO to update the CEO and executive committee."

Expected response: A QSBR deck structure tracking strategic initiative milestones, talent metric trends, resource investments, and emerging execution risks for C-suite alignment.

## Workflow summary

The CHRO conducts rigorous environmental scanning, designs a 3-year business-aligned HR strategic plan with clear KPIs, and institutes quarterly executive review governance to ensure execution velocity.
