# Aligning Post-Merger Operating Models and Culture

## Context

Following the merger of two regional healthcare organizations with 4,000 combined employees, the combined leadership team faces severe culture clashes, conflicting HR systems, and overlapping management structures. The Head of HR Integration is tasked with designing the 100-day HR post-merger integration plan.

## Step 1: Ensure Day 1 operational readiness

Sample prompt: "Build a Day 1 HR integration checklist covering payroll continuity, benefits enrollment, leadership announcements, and employee onboarding."

Expected response: A Day 1 readiness checklist verifying payroll synchronization, dual-brand internal communications, interim reporting lines, and employee support hotlines.

## Step 2: Harmonize compensation, benefits, and org structure

Sample prompt: "Draft a post-merger compensation and benefits harmonization strategy aligning two different grade structures and incentive plans."

Expected response: A phased harmonization plan aligning salary bands, health benefits, and sales incentive plans without triggering voluntary turnover among key revenue-generating talent.

## Step 3: Track 100-day integration milestones and synergies

Sample prompt: "Create a 100-day HR integration roadmap tracking talent selection, organizational alignment, system migration, and culture integration."

Expected response: A comprehensive 100-day roadmap tracking workstream progress across HRIS consolidation, joint leadership alignment workshops, and synergy realization targets.

## Workflow summary

The HR Integration Lead secures Day 1 operational continuity, systematically harmonizes compensation and benefits structures, and tracks 100-day integration milestones to build a unified organizational culture.
