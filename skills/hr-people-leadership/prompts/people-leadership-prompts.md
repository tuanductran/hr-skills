# People leadership prompts

Use these prompts when developing people leadership capability, coaching individual managers, or designing enterprise-wide leadership frameworks and culture.

- "Define the people leadership expectations for managers at [level — team lead, manager, director] at [company] — including the specific behaviors that distinguish excellent people leaders from average managers in this context."
- "Write a development plan for a first-time manager who has just transitioned from individual contributor to people leader — covering the mindset shifts, highest-risk mistakes, and the skills to build in the first 90 days."
- "Coach a senior leader who leads primarily through authority and urgency rather than trust and influence — what coaching questions help them recognize the impact of their leadership style on team psychological safety?"
- "Design a people leadership competency framework for [company] covering [team lead to VP] levels — including observable behaviors for each competency at each level."
- "Write a feedback script an HRBP can use to deliver honest, direct feedback to a manager about a leadership behavior that is damaging team trust — without triggering defensiveness."
- "What metrics and signals indicate a manager is not meeting people leadership expectations — and how do we distinguish a manager who is struggling from one who is the wrong fit for the role?"
- "Design a peer learning program for [number] managers to learn from each other's people leadership experiences — including session structure, facilitation approach, and how to make the learning transfer to day-to-day practice."
- "How do we build a consistent people leadership culture across [multiple teams / geographies / business units] where managers have very different styles and the organization has no shared leadership norms?"
- "Design an inclusive leadership workshop for [manager population] covering the specific behaviors that create or undermine psychological safety, belonging, and equitable opportunity for team members."
- "How do we hold leaders accountable for creating inclusive and high-trust team environments in performance reviews — not just for business delivery results?"
