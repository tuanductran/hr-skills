# People leadership development

Build and strengthen people leadership capability across the organization — from coaching individual managers on core leadership practices to designing enterprise-wide people leadership frameworks, assessment tools, and development programs.

## Supported tasks

- Defining people leadership standards and expectations
- Coaching managers on core leadership behaviors
- Designing leadership capability frameworks and competencies
- Creating feedback and accountability conversations
- Building psychological safety and inclusive team climates
- Assessing people leadership effectiveness and impact
- Designing people leadership development programs
- Supporting new manager transitions and first-time leaders
- Building leadership accountability for team health and performance
- Designing leadership 360-degree feedback processes
- Developing leadership culture and shared norms
- Supporting senior leaders in modeling people leadership behaviors

## Key prompts

### Leadership standards and expectations

1. "Define the people leadership expectations for managers at [level] in [company]."
2. "What behaviors distinguish excellent people leaders from average managers in [industry or function]?"
3. "Write a People Leadership Charter for [company] that describes what we expect from every manager."
4. "How do we translate our company values into specific people leadership behaviors?"
5. "Design a people leadership competency framework for [company] covering [levels from team lead to VP]."

### Coaching and development

1. "Coach a manager who is technically strong but struggles to build trust with their team."
2. "Write a development plan for a first-time manager transitioning from individual contributor to people leader."
3. "What coaching questions help a manager who avoids difficult feedback conversations start having them?"
4. "Design a 90-day people leadership development plan for a newly promoted [team lead / manager / director]."
5. "How do we coach a senior leader who leads through authority and status rather than influence and trust?"
6. "What leadership behaviors should we reinforce versus address in [manager's] six-month review?"

### Feedback and accountability

1. "Write a feedback script for giving a manager honest feedback about their team leadership style."
2. "Design a leadership accountability framework that ensures managers are held responsible for team health."
3. "How do we create a culture where managers receive upward feedback regularly and act on it?"
4. "Write a structured conversation guide for HR to use when coaching a manager through a team health issue."
5. "What metrics and signals indicate a manager is not meeting people leadership expectations?"

### Psychological safety and inclusion

1. "What practices help managers build psychological safety in [remote / hybrid / high-pressure] teams?"
2. "How do we assess the level of psychological safety in [team or department] and diagnose root causes?"
3. "What behaviors undermine psychological safety and how do we coach managers to stop them?"
4. "Design an inclusive leadership workshop for [manager population] covering [key behaviors]."
5. "How do we hold leaders accountable for creating inclusive team environments, not just diverse headcount?"

### Leadership culture

1. "How do we build a consistent people leadership culture across [multiple teams / geographies / business units]?"
2. "Design a peer learning program for managers to learn from each other's people leadership experiences."
3. "What rituals and forums reinforce strong people leadership norms across [organization]?"
4. "How do we identify and develop informal leaders who influence team culture without formal authority?"
5. "What signals tell us our people leadership culture is improving at an organizational level?"

## Tips

- The quality of people leadership in an organization is the single biggest driver of engagement, performance, and retention — treat it as a strategic priority, not a soft skill.
- New manager transitions are the highest-risk leadership moment; a structured 90-day program pays for itself by preventing early team trust breakdowns.
- Most managers avoid difficult conversations because they lack confidence, not because they don't care — build skills and scripts, not just motivation.
- 360-degree feedback is only valuable when leaders receive it in a psychologically safe context with coaching support; feedback without support often creates defensiveness.
- Senior leader modeling matters more than any training program — if the top of the house doesn't demonstrate strong people leadership, no program will change the culture below.
