# HR risk management prompts

Use these prompts when identifying, assessing, or mitigating people-related risks across the organization.

- "Identify the top 10 HR risks for a [industry/company stage] organization and categorize them by compliance, operational, people, reputational, and strategic risk."
- "Create an HR risk register template covering risk category, description, likelihood (1-5), impact (1-5), risk owner, current controls, and mitigation actions."
- "Conduct a people risk assessment for a company undergoing [rapid growth / restructure / acquisition] — identify which HR processes and policies are most exposed."
- "Write a workforce key-person dependency risk map identifying roles where departure would cause significant disruption, and recommend mitigation actions."
- "Build a grievance and misconduct risk response protocol, covering escalation levels, investigation triggers, documentation standards, and timeline expectations."
- "Design an HR compliance monitoring calendar for [labor law area / country] covering which compliance checks to run and at what frequency."
- "Write an investigation protocol for handling a [harassment / misconduct / grievance] complaint, including intake, investigation steps, outcome, and documentation."
- "Develop an early warning system for identifying flight-risk employees — what signals to track, how to act on them, and how to keep the process confidential."
- "Create a data privacy risk control checklist for HR processes, covering employee data collection, storage, access, retention, and cross-border transfer."
- "Design a quarterly HR risk review process for senior leadership — format, agenda, escalation criteria, and how to connect risk findings to business decisions."
